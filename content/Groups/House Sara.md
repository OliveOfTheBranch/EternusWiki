House Sara are the royal house of the [[Sara Dominion]]. The first of the line was [[Non-Player Characters/Clio Sara]], who upon slaying the god [[Typhon]] ("The Predator") ascended to become a goddess herself. A fragment of her power is said to run through their veins. 

The current ruling king and head of house is [[Non-Player Characters/Theodore Sara]]. 

Their symbol is a bow and arrow, and their words are "Blessed is the Hunt".
