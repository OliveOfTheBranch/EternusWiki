###### Demiplane
The Shrike's Tine is a [[Demiplane]] created by [[Non-Player Characters/Britta of Shrike]] during the early [[Events/Golden Age]]. It serves as a secure location to store valuable artifacts and information, as well as a place of peace and concentration. 

The Tine belongs to [[Groups/House Mentis]], and can only be accessed by worthy oracles of Britta's line. The current owner of the Shrike’s Tine is [[Player Characters/Xenevieve Mentis-Sara]]. 

The [[Tinekeeper]], an artificial general created by Britta, protects, organises, and preserves the artifacts stored there.
