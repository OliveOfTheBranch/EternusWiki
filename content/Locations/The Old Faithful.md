A bar in the Downtown "Belly" District of [[Locations/Chimaera]]. Its proprietor is [[Daenerys Fletcher]]. The Old Faithful's basement parlour is occasionally used to host events for [[Groups/House Orion]], usually arranged by [[Charity Orion]]. [[Player Characters/Xenevieve Sara]] and [[Non-Player Characters/Theodore Sara]] were known to frequent the bar, likely due to Daenerys dating Xenavieve's housekeeper, [[Cypress of Saturnalia]].
