###### (he/him) - NPC
[[Xaeres]]’s protégé, Carter is in charge of keeping the [[Locations/Godflow Engine]] running, as well as trapping any who disobey [[Non-Player Characters/Tasha]]’s orders in ‘the Maze’, an infinite spiral of liminal caverns. He is also known as the Grazed One.

His sister is [[Kairo of Marwin]], a member of [[Eternus Victoria]].
