###### Region
In the aftermath of [[Events/Agon#The Second Agon\|The Second Agon]], [[Groups/The Triad]] grew angry that the mortals had slain so many of their siblings, and united together to destroy two of the great cities of [[Locations/Eternus]], [[Locations/Arkas]] and [[Sigil]]. They attacked Arkas first, giving Sigil time to save itself by lifting into the air. When the Triad struck Sigil, they were too late, and their attack fell upon the ground below, creating the area known as Landfall.

The area is now a large desert of black sand. It was rumoured that [[The Devourer]], and elder evil, was sealed in the centre of this desert, and that [[Non-Player Characters/Corellon Larethian]] went there to destroy it for good. The adventuring party [[Zhetrics]] confirmed this after going to Landfall to defeat both the Devourer and a corrupted Corellon.
