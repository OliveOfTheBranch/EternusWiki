###### Disaster
The Great Fire of Chimaera was a terrible event that occurred in 688 GA. A fire was started by [[Non-Player Characters/Adonis Mentis]], the oracle at the time, and quickly grew out of control, spreading throughout the city of [[Locations/Chimaera]] and causing great damage. The fire allowed Adonis to see a great prophecy, though he was stripped of his title and banished from the city shortly after the event.
