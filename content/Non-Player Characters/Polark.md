###### (he/it/she\*) - NPC
The primordial god of darkness and the abyss, Pölark is a cloaked god known to wear many faces. In some places, it is worshipped as creator of [[The Abyss]], yet in others sailors pray to her for a safe journey across the sea. It created demons, and for a long time battled in a “[[Blood War]]” with [[Non-Player Characters/Lathander]]. Now, it is a member of [[The Triad]], consisting of itself, [[Non-Player Characters/Lathander]], and [[Titania]].
