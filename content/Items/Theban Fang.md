###### Artefact
The Theban Fang is the artifact of [[Groups/House Cadmus]]. 

It was taken by [[Archprior Cadmus]] from an [[Elder Dragons\|Elder Dragon]] that she slayed prior to [[Events/Agon#The First Agon\|The First Agon]]. This violent act caused her to be exiled from her home in [[Locations/Felor]], leading her to take up residence in the [[Hunting Grounds]], where she was eventually sought out by [[Non-Player Characters/Clio Sara]], and joined the [[Groups/Hunters of Sara]]. 

The Theban Fang is used in a ritual known as [[Magic/The Divine Rites#Vox Sowing\|Vox Sowing]], where the new head of House Cadmus must pierce the fang into their throat, and use the [[Languages/Old Tongue]] to stop the bleeding. If successful, the fang remains lodged in place, and the user is able to speak the Old Tongue with perfect fluency, though loses all other speech.
