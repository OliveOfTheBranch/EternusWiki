###### Fey
Eladrin are a species of [[Creatures/Fey]] that are tied closely to the seasons of [[Locations/Eternus]]. As the weather begins to shift, eladrin change with it, swapping out their appearance, powers, and even personality. 

There are four eladrin [[Creatures/Archfey]], each connected to their own season. These archfey are:
- [[Bacchus]]
- [[Oberon]]
- [[Auster]]
- [[Auril]]
