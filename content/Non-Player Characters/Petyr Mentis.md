###### (he/him) - NPC
Petyr Mentis was an elf from the [[Locations/Sara Dominion]]. He and his sister [[Non-Player Characters/Cordelia Mentis]] were raised in [[Locations/Port Kal'Vess]] by their father, [[Non-Player Characters/Caius Mentis]], but moved to their home in [[Locations/Chimaera]] once they were older. Only a few years later, their cousin, [[Non-Player Characters/Lily Mentis]], moved to Chimaera as well, and the three lived together while a new Mentis property began its construction. Petyr was able to use leylines to see the future, taking frequent trips to the [[Terrania Gorge]]. Cordelia and Petyr, as well as their cousin Lily, were all killed in the same incident involving [[Non-Player Characters/Desdemona Mentis]]. 

Petyr could read the future through the shifting of leylines, especially visible in places such as Terrania Gorge.
