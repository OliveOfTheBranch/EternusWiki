###### Noble House
House Cadmus is the house of priors in the [[Locations/Sara Dominion]]. They are descended from [[Archprior Cadmus]], who slayed an elder dragon and learned to fluently speak the [[Languages/Old Tongue]].

Their family artefact is the [[Items/Theban Fang]]. The current head of house is [[Non-Player Characters/Eadaz Cadmus]], who is also the prioress of [[The Priory]]. She has undergone the ritual of "[[Magic/The Divine Rites#Vox Sowing\|Vox Sowing]]", forcing her to exclusively speak the Old Tongue with perfect fluency, and coronating her as the prioress under King [[Non-Player Characters/Theodore Sara]].

House Cadmus is based in [[Locations/Chimaera]].

Their symbol is a dragon, and their words are "Sow the spoils."
