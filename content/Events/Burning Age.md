###### Age
The Burning Age is the second recorded age of [[Locations/Eternus]], coming after the [[Events/Golden Age]], and lasting 214 years. The Age began with the [[Fall of Arkas]] and the displacement of [[Locations/Sigil]], followed by the [[Events/Time of Strife]], a century of devastation that affected all of Eternus. [[Locations/Ethrys]] suffered mostly from crop failure, while [[Locations/Alfheim]] was subject to a variety of issues caused by the displacement of Sigil, and subsequent creation of [[Locations/Landfall]] that allowed monsters such as [[Nightshades]] to freely roam the continent. 

The Burning Age ended when [[Non-Player Characters/Tasha]] performed the [[Rending Ritual]], creating an artificial [[Events/Agon]] that began the [[Events/Current Age]].
