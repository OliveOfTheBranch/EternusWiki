###### (she/her) - NPC
Austra Nemea is the current head of [[Groups/House Nemea]] in the [[Locations/Sara Dominion]]. While Austra may be the eldest of the current leading nobles, she has a significant amount of power, and represents the South in a way that none of the other southern houses can. Her mood can be rather changeable as time wears her down, but her opinions are never to be ignored.
