###### Pantheon
The Morteus Gods are a subset of [[Groups/The Twelve Who Came Before]], comprising of the gods who held dominion over the lives, and more often deaths, of mortals. These gods were:
- [[Non-Player Characters/The Sanguine Lord]]
- [[Non-Player Characters/Octavian]]
- [[Non-Player Characters/Typhon]]
- [[Non-Player Characters/The Wanderer]]

The Morteus Gods were often the most direct with their influence, sometimes even appearing as an avatar to wreak destruction (especially in the case of Typhon).
