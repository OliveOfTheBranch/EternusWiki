###### Country
The Ashen Empire is a group of volcanic islands just south of [[Locations/Ethrys]]. They were settled by the people of [[Yuphral]] and [[Locations/Felor]] sometime before [[Events/Agon#The First Agon\|The First Agon]], and were considered, much like Felor, to be a part of Yuphral. 

The people of the Ashen Empire worship [[Groups/The Twelve Who Came Before]], and do not consider the usurper gods to be worthy of worship. This view caused them to declare independence from Yuphral after the second agon when Yuphral shifted to worshipping [[Non-Player Characters/Alexandria]]. They now govern themselves, and live undisturbed on their islands.
