The Outer Realm is a space that exists outside of [[Locations/Eternus]], beyond the [[Magic/Veil]]. It is a dark, lawless void filled with roiling [[Magic/Cardinal]], where time and space mean nothing, and the only source of light is the golden glow of fleeing gods' [[Magic/Animus]]. [[Groups/The Twelve Who Came Before]] originated in the Outer Realm, and travelled to Eternus while being pursued by a [[Creatures/Godeater]], the great predators of the realm. When the Veil split open during [[Events/Agon#The Rending\|The Rending]], it was that same Godeater that extended its reach to [[Non-Player Characters/Tasha]], [[Non-Player Characters/Týr Sara]], and [[Player Characters/Xenevieve Mentis-Sara\|Xenevieve Mentis]]. 

Entities that come from the Outer Realm are known as elder evils, and are amalgamations of Cardinal energy. While the most notable of these has been the Godeater, there are also many other manifestations, notably including:
- [[Illithids]]
- [[Snarks]]
