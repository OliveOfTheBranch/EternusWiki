###### (he/him) - NPC
Eitr Sara is a minor god from the northern Hunting Grounds. He is also known as Python, Jörmungandr, or Zehir, depending on where he is worshipped.

He has the ability to transform into a snake, and is worshipped as a god of snakes, poison, and protection. 

His younger sister, [[Xanthe Sara]], died from the pipes when she was young, leaving Eitr with only his brother, [[Non-Player Characters/Týr Sara]]. When the two eventually split paths, Eitr found his way to the young city of [[Locations/Arkas]], and took the form of an enormous serpent in order to wrap around the entire city and protect it from harm. When the [[Groups/Hunters of Sara]] heard news of his presence, [[Non-Player Characters/Aureon Mentis\|Saint Mentis]] and [[Saint Orion]] journeyed out to Arkas in order to cast him away from the city, freeing up the trade routes from north to south. 

Eitr fled southeast, breaking through the cliffs and creating [[Locations/Serpent's Folly]], before disappearing into the sea. He spent the next several hundred years on the [[Isle of the Three Sisters]], protecting it from being found. It was there that he met [[Lliira]], the goddess of Joy, who he eventually married. 
During [[Events/Agon#The Rending\|The Rending]], Eitr joined forces with [[Player Characters/Xenevieve Mentis-Sara]] and [[Groups/The Pack]], taking up temporary residence in [[Locations/Chimaera]].
