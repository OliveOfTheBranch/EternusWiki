###### (he/they) - NPC
Ilya Ceryn is the current head of [[Groups/House Ceryn]]. He was a [[Creatures/Changelings\|Changeling]] born in the [[Locations/Feywild]], but was adopted by Lord and Lady Ceryn, who brought him back to the [[Locations/Sara Dominion]] and raised him to be their heir. 

Ilya was the champion of House Ceryn during King [[Non-Player Characters/Theodore Sara]]'s coronation.
