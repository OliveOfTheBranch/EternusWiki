###### Cardinal Armament
Snark is a magical sword currently owned by [[Player Characters/Vuris Sanavaloth]]. 

The creature within the sword sharing its name was summoned from the [[Locations/Outer Realm]] by [[Non-Player Characters/Titania]], and given physical form as a [[Creatures/Fey]]. It once roamed the [[Locations/Feywild]] freely, and spent much time in [[Marwin]]. It is a [[Magic/Cardinal]] entity, so far beyond comprehension that it can only be seen outside of its sword form by young children, whose imaginations know no bounds. 

It was bound to a sword by [[Non-Player Characters/Tasha]], and remained with her in the Palace of Heart's Desire in [[Locations/Prismeer]], until it was stolen by Vuris during [[Events/Agon#The Rending\|The Rending]].
