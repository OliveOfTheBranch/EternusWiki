###### City
Crux is a city in central [[Locations/Yuphral]], and is considered by most to be the oldest human city in [[Locations/Eternus]]. 

The city was built by early Yuphrali people, who worshipped the god [[Non-Player Characters/Yuphra]]. In modern day, the citizens of Crux worship [[Non-Player Characters/Alexandria]], and in fact destroyed many symbols of Yuphra and its worship after [[Events/Agon#The Second Agon\|The Second Agon]].
