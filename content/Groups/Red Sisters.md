###### Religious Organisation
The Red Sisters are a group of [[Locations/Dreygaard\|Dregaardian]] nuns who worship, serve, and sometimes represent [[Non-Player Characters/The Sanguine Lord]]. As the Sanguine Lord is a dead god, the Red Sisters are tasked with the role of interpreting his will and sharing it with the people of Dreygaard. 

They often serve as medics, midwives, and church staff, though the nunnery offers a wide range of options for young Sisters to train themselves in. The girls serving in these nunneries are often criminals, offered a second chance by the church. They are legally owned by the Cardinal (currently [[Non-Player Characters/Gisela of Rott]]), and spend the remainder of their lives serving the Red Sisters, though varying levels of freedom may be achieved. 

Inside the nunneries, Sisters are given free food and accommodations, as well as a variety of classes to prepare them for their future roles. While this provides them with opportunities that may have been previously unavailable, it is important to note that the protection of the church is conditional, and that many Sisters have been known to disappear after causing too much trouble.
