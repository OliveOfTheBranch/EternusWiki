###### (he/him) - NPC
Favon Atalanta is the current head of [[Groups/House Atalanta]]. 

He is the Warden of the West, and serves as the leader of the [[Oldegate]] region. Favon is a half-elf, and is fluent in [[Saran]], [[Languages/Elvish]], and [[Languages/Sylvan]], the common languages of Oldegate. He and his wife, [[Cil'ahn of Shrike]], spend most of their time in Oldegate, though travel frequently to [[Locations/Chimaera]] for business. 

Favon was the champion of House Atalanta during King Theodore’s coronation.
