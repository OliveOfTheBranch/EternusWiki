###### Group
The Pack is an ancient group of lycanthropes (specifically [[Werewolves]]) led by [[Non-Player Characters/Týr Sara]], the Fenrisúlfr. His deputies, and leaders of the Pack in his absence, are [[Hati]] and [[Non-Player Characters/Sköll]], the Mooneater and Suneater respectively.
#### Notable Members
- [[Non-Player Characters/Týr Sara]], founder, leader, and Lord of Wolves
- [[Hati]], deputy and ex-leader
- [[Non-Player Characters/Sköll]], deputy and ex-leader
- [[Player Characters/Xenevieve Sara]], ex-Duchess of Wolves, honourary
- [[Ylga]], honourary
- Jarred, Bonesmith
