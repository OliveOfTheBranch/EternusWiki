###### Disease
Greenlung is a respiratory condition most commonly found in the city of [[Sugadinti]]. It affects [[Items/Dimeritium]] miners who breathe in large quantities of dimeritium dust while working in [[Locations/Pandemonium]]. The green particles adhere to the lungs, restricting oxygen and sapping away at the afflicted individual's energy, and in spellcasters interrupting their connection with the [[Magic/Veil]]. Eventually, if left untreated, this condition leads to death.
