###### (he/him) - NPC
Týr Sara is a human werewolf from the [[Locations/Sara Dominion]], and false god of lycanthropy and retribution. He is also known as Fenrir, the Fenrisúlfr, Romulus, or simply the Wolf.

Týr is tall and muscular, but in a way that makes him look frail at first, where there is no fat between the toughened muscle and skin. His features are unnaturally sharp, as if each pore of his skin is attempting to push itself away from the bone. His right eye is missing, and is instead a gaping hole from which smoke emerges.

Týr is the father of [[Non-Player Characters/Clio Sara]], but only raised her in early childhood.
