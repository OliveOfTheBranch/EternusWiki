###### (he/him) - NPC
Dalys Meleager was the previous head of [[Groups/House Meleager]] in the [[Locations/Sara Dominion]]. 
After the death of King [[Non-Player Characters/Orion Sara]] in the [[Events/Battle of Oldegate]], Dalys became regent of the Sara Dominion, ruling for 18 years between 196 BA and 214 BA, until King [[Non-Player Characters/Theodore Sara]] was old enough to ascend the throne.

Dalys married a Hesperidean woman named Isi, and had a son, [[Jason Meleager]]. After King Theodore took the throne, Dalys retired from his noble duties, and named his sister, [[Sana Meleager]], the new head of House Meleager.
