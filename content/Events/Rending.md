The Rending was an event that occurred in 214CA. It is sometimes called the Third [[Events/Agon]], due to its similarity to Agonic events, despite not technically being an Agon at all. The Rending was initiated by [[Tasha]], under the guise of the Archfey [[Zybilna]], as an attempt for her to start an Agon so that she could harvest more [[Magic/Animus]] to place within the [[Godflow Engine]].
