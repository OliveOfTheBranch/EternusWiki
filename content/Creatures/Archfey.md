###### Fey
An Archfey is any [[Creatures/Fey]] that has control over a domain in the [[Locations/Feywild]]. 

This position is achieved in various ways depending on the specific domain and their traditions. For example, in [[Locations/Shrike]], the title of Archfey is given to anyone who can defeat the current Archfey in one on one combat. Typically, the position and powers of being an Archfey come with being the most powerful fey living in a given domain. Domains that have their Archfey permanently leave or die appoint a new one through the land's inherent fey magic.

A list of Archfey and their respective domains in given on the [[Locations/Feywild]] page.
