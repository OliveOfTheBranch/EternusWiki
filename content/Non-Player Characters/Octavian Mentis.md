###### (he/him) - NPC
Octavian Mentis was a half-Elf from the [[Locations/Sara Dominion]]. He took his mother’s title after she retired, and moved to [[Locations/Arkas]], while his younger brother, [[Non-Player Characters/Caius Mentis]], moved to the coast with his mother to relax after the war. Like his [[Non-Player Characters/Octavian\|namesake]], Octavian’s power was linked closely with death as his oracular abilities allowed him to know when people would die. It was theorised, long after his death, that it may have been Arkas’s grim future that drew him to live there in the first place. Due to council pressure, he eventually retired from his position, passing the title to his daughter, [[Non-Player Characters/Lily Mentis]]. He planted a field of lilies outside of Arkas a year before his daughter’s death, and disappeared shortly after. 

Octavian was able to predict when people would die. 

He was oracle from 572 GA - 606 GA (34 years).
