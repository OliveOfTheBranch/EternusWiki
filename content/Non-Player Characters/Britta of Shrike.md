###### (she/her) - NPC
Britta of Shrike was the [[Archfey]] of [[Shrike]] around the time of [[Events/Agon#The First Agon\|The First Agon]]. She is also known as Brittany Mentis. She is well known for creating the first fey gateway in [[Locations/Ethrys]], located in [[Locations/Oldegate]], a city that she founded between the western mountains. 

While in Ethrys, Britta met [[Non-Player Characters/Aureon Mentis]], the Oracle of Sara, and the two were eventually married, Britta being the one to create the name "Mentis", from the [[Languages/Sylvan]] words "of the mind". They had one daughter, [[Non-Player Characters/Riparia Mentis]], after which, Aureon returned to his home in [[Locations/Arkas]], leaving Britta to rule [[Groups/House Mentis]] on her own. Britta had a close relationship with [[Saint Atalanta]], as the two both claimed a sort of leadership over the Oldegate region. She created the [[Rose of Skull and Stone]] from the bones of [[Ursa]], which became the artifact of [[Groups/House Atalanta]]. 

Britta also created [[The Shrike’s Tine]], a demiplane that could only be accessed by a worthy oracle of her line. It has been used to store centuries worth of prophecies, artifacts, and significant Mentis belongings. An artificial general known as the [[Tinekeeper]] serves as the protector of the Tine, and works to organise and preserve the contents left there by oracles of the past and present. The Tinekeeper was created from river stones by Britta, and still protects her legacy to this day. 
Eventually, Britta grew too powerful, and it was decided that she needed to be defeated. Ten of the twelve [[Groups/Hunters of Sara]] gathered together for a final hunt, and ultimately managed to kill her.
