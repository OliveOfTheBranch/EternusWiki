###### Artefact
The Mask of Ladon is an artifact of [[Groups/House Meleager]], designed during the Saran Civil War by [[Castor Meleager]], and [[Non-Player Characters/Cressida Mentis]]. It was made to be an extension of the powers granted by the [[Orb of Ladon]], allowing [[Non-Player Characters/Ladon]] to possess the wearer and use his powers through them. The wearer may teleport at will and gains Ladon's speed and strength in combat. The mask can only be used by Meleagers.
