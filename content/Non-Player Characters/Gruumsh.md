###### (he/him) - NPC
The god of violence and murder, Gruumsh was an ogrelord of [[Acheron]] when he killed [[Non-Player Characters/Octavian]] in cold blood, finding himself to be a god through sheer coincidence alone. The Ruiner is known for his cruelty, having placed a curse of savagery on his legion of [[Orcs]] to give them an insatiable bloodlust. Those who are able to fight the curse renounce him as a god and flee from his iron fist.
