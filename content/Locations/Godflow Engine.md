The godflow engine is a colossal series of golden pylons surrounding [[Locations/Carceri]] that draw [[Non-Player Characters/The Wanderer]]'s [[Magic/Animus]] from the prison plane and feed it to the flying city of [[Locations/Sigil]], which gives [[Groups/The Court of Pain]] their divine powers. Its interior is controlled by the Grazed Sovereign [[Non-Player Characters/Carter of Marwin]], who ensures that the animus cannot leave, while the pylons themself are controlled by [[Non-Player Characters/Aureon Mentis]], the Sovereign of Knowledge.
