###### Fey Domain
Prismeer is a realm in the [[Locations/Feywild]]. It was ruled by the [[Archfey]], Zybilna, who was also known as [[Non-Player Characters/Tasha]], or Iggwilv. Prismeer has three distinct regions, as well as a central area where Zybilna lived in her Palace of Heart’s Desire. These regions are the swamps of Hither, the forests of Thither, and the stormy cliffs of Yon. 

Shortly before [[Events/Agon#The Rending\|The Rending]], Zybilna was frozen by three hags: [[Bavlorna Blightstraw]], [[Skabatha Nightshade]], and [[Endelyn Moongrave]], who took control of the three realms of Prismeer. The hag’s reign continued until they were defeated by [[Groups/Eternus Victoria]], who freed Zybilna. 
The [[Rending Ritual]] was performed by Zybilna in the Palace of Heart’s Desire.
