###### City
Serpent's Folly is a coastal city in the southern [[Locations/Sara Dominion]]. The location it is in was created when the great serpent [[Non-Player Characters/Eitr Sara\|Python]] fled from [[Locations/Arkas]] after being defeated by [[Non-Player Characters/Aureon Mentis\|The Oracle of Sara]]. Gravely wounded, it slid off a crumbling cliffside which fell around it into the sea. This created an anchorage in a previously unreachable area, where settlers moved to once water started flowing through it and out to sea from the newly formed River Python. The city sits at the top of the cliff, while the crumbled edge falls into the anchorage below.

A highlight of the city is the system of pullies, hooks, and cranes used to pull both cargo and whole ships from the water and up to the river such that they could sail down to Arkas. After the Fall of Arkas, boats no longer sail up the river, but the pully system has been redesigned to pull cargo from the anchorage to the top of the cliff where it can be placed in warehouses or carted to Bas'Taurus.

After the Fall of Arkas, the water of the River Python was polluted. It now contains magical properties, and while it is safe to drink, it has been known to spark wild magic surges in spellcasters.
