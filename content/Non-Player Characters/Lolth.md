###### (she/her) - NPC
The goddess of lies and spiders, Lolth was once the captain of Mab’s guard, before she betrayed and slaughtered her during the Second Agon, and in doing so sent the Unseelie of the Feywild into mayhem. Claiming the Unseelie Crown, Lolth gave all those who once worshipped Mab a choice: convert or die. Those who weren’t drawn into the Abyss by Lolth’s silver tongue or murdered by her forces went into voluntary exile, forced to wander as nomads.
