###### Religious Text
The Predator's Bible is a religious text written by [[Saint Orion]], [[Non-Player Characters/Aureon Mentis\|Saint Mentis]], and King [[Ulysses Sara]], describing the events of the first agon from the perspective of the [[Groups/Hunters of Sara]]. It tells the story of the Goddess, [[Non-Player Characters/Clio Sara\|Kleos]], who gathered the greatest hunters from across the Hunting Grounds, and slayed the God, [[Non-Player Characters/Typhon]], becoming the first mortal to achieve true godhood. 

The Predator’s Bible was rewritten by Saint Orion sometime during the early [[Events/Golden Age]].
