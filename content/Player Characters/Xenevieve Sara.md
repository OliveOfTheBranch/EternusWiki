###### (she/her) - Played by Zae
Queen Xenevieve Sara (née [[House Mentis|Mentis]]) is the current ruling queen consort of the [[Sara Dominion]]. She is a high elf, and is also known as Vivi, or the "[[Cardinal]] Queen".

## Description
#### Appearance
Vivi has long blond ringlets. Her right eye is missing, a dark gaping hole.
#### Personality
Vivi can be somewhat intimidating to be in a room with, though is joyful when surrounded by those she trusts.
## Biography
She is married to King [[Theodore Sara]], and has five children by him, being [[Anastasia Sara]], [[Victoria Sara]], [[Apollo Sara]], [[Artemis Sara]], and [[Python Sara]].
#### Background
Not long after Xenevieve was born into House Mentis in [[Chimaera]], her [[Pythia Mentis|mother]] passed away of an unexplainable illness. Not long after, into her teens, her [[Croesus Mentis|father]] died of [[Spellrot|spellrot]]. Running away from home, she joined an adventuring party known as [[Eternus Victoria]], which led her into the [[Feywild]] in search of her lost friends. Upon returning to Chimaera, she married King Theo and ascended to the throne.
