###### Chimaera
Capras are a species of large, goat-like [[Creatures/Chimaera (Creatures)\|Chimaera]] native to the [[Locations/Sara Dominion]]. They are one of the few chimaera to have been successfully farmed by humans, and were used as livestock, as well as beasts of burden. Capra live mostly in open plains, and graze on grass and wildflowers. 

Sometimes, the flowers that they eat can attract bees to live in their stomachs, forming a symbiotic relationship. These specific capra are often referred to as capra hives, or simply hives, and are prized for their rare honey and [[Items/Aether]] bezoars.
