###### (she/her) - NPC
Marrissa Erymr is the current head of [[Groups/House Erymr]]. She is also known as Mars. She is half dwarven, and married to [[Lochlan Erymr]], a dwarf from [[Rymrdann]]. 

During [[Events/Agon#The Rending\|The Rending]], Marissa allied herself with Queen [[Player Characters/Xenevieve Sara]], working closely with her and several others to get King [[Non-Player Characters/Theodore Sara]] in power. As the head of defence, she also helped greatly during conflicts with [[Locations/Dreygaard]].
