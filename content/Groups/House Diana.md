###### Noble House
House Diana is the royal guard and keeper of hounds in the [[Locations/Sara Dominion]]. They are descended from [[Saint Diana]], who was the daughter of the King of the North, and therefore the brother of [[Ulysses Sara]].

Their family artefact is the Bone Whistle. The current head of house and captain of the royal guard is [[Sirius Diana]], with the master of hounds being his older sister, [[Non-Player Characters/Nemorensis Diana]].

House Diana is based in [[Locations/Chimaera]].

Their symbol is a hound, and their words are "Purity is the fortress."
