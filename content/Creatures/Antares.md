###### Chimaera General
Antares was a [[Creatures/Chimaera (Creatures)\|Chimaera]] general in the Hunting Grounds of East [[Locations/Ethrys]], and took the form of a scorpion. Unlike other chimaera of its time, Antares did not hunt in the traditional fashion, and instead lured its prey into the ancient city of [[Locations/Archela]] in the south of what is now modern day [[Locations/Sara Dominion]]. It used its mind altering venom to keep the citizens of Archela under its control, maintaining a steady population and enjoying easy prey without fear of being hunted. Eventually it was defeated by [[Saint Orion]], who later joined the [[Groups/Hunters of Sara]], and its tail was utilised for making the Spear of Antares.
