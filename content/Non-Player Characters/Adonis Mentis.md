###### (he/him) - NPC
Adonis Mentis was an elf from the [[Locations/Sara Dominion]]. After taking her position by force, [[Non-Player Characters/Desdemona Mentis]] had three children: [[Non-Player Characters/Lorelei Mentis]], [[Non-Player Characters/Helena Mentis]], and Adonis. Lorelei heard whispers of the future whenever the world was silent, Helena could tell the future using a set of carved bones, and Adonis saw visions flickering in fire. Adonis’s older sister, Helena, declined the position of oracle after Lorelei was diagnosed with [[Magic/Spellrot]] and retired, leaving Adonis to inherit the position. As part of a major prophecy, he started the [[Great Fire of Chimaera]], leading to one of the greatest prophecies in a century. He was removed from the position, and moved to [[Regulus]], where he married a human, and had one son, [[Non-Player Characters/Elio Mentis]]. 

Adonis saw visions flickering in fire.

He was oracle from 671 GA to 688 GA (17 years).
