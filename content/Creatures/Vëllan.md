The Vëllan are an indigenous population of [[Locations/Ethrys]], native to northern [[Locations/Dreygaard]] and the [[Locations/Vëlheim\|Vëlheim Isles]].

They are characterised by their vibrant red hair, and often bear many piercings and tattoos due to religious reasons associated with their creator, [[Non-Player Characters/The Wanderer]], god of pain. They are well known in Dreygaard for their use of witchcraft, which has led to the formation of the [[Groups/Vëlwathe]] who regularly embark on witch hunting expeditions to Vëlheim.
