The South sees Theo coming into power as a puppet of House Meleager coming into power. Even if Dalys isn't still the regent, his keeping Theo in hiding means that he has essentially ruled the country and been the sole keeper of the king's ear for 18 years. Now, when Theo ascends to the throne, the South believes he will follow Dalys's orders blindly.
The South sees House Meleager as traitors to the North (which has been a theme for a long time). In power, Dalys has repeatedly avoided interest in the South to improve negotiations in the North, leaving the South distrusting of him.
Austra Nemea is old, and once she dies Leon Nemea will come into power. He is likely to call for the separation of North and South because of the above. Regulus is incredibly loyal to House Nemea, so they will support him in this. If Regulus goes, Archela will follow, causing House Orion to back the cause. Once this happens, a land border forms on the River Python that cuts off the North from the South. If House Meleager doesn't give up Cassowary and the Hesperides, the chances that the Daymora Khaganate invades it, seizing the opportunity to gain more land for the Western Khanate, are high.
None of this happens if an Orion becomes advisor.
The attack was only meant to be a pressuring on Orion grounds, though a miscommunication alongside an overzealous hitman lead to the situation escalating.
This situation is bad for House Taurus as the South splitting loses the North its trade with the Daymora Khaganate. As the North then has virtually no trading neighbours, it will begin to starve and revolt. With no heir to the throne and pressure already high, the situation would be a threat to House Sara and the Dominion as a whole. Taurus recognises the fact the a whole Sara Dominion is best for everyone, not just the South, and therefore in their best interests.
