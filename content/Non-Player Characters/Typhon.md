###### (he/it) - NPC
The primordial god of the hunt, Typhon was an apex predator and creator of the first monsters, known as chimaera. He roamed the Hunting Grounds of East Ethrys, wreaking carnage and destruction in his wake, until he himself was hunted and killed by the mortal [[Non-Player Characters/Clio Sara]] and her [[Groups/Hunters of Sara\|hunters]]. He was part of the [[Groups/Morteus Gods]].

Typhon's avatar appeared as a huge hydra-like entity, yet it was able to change its form at will to fit the surrounding environment. When he was hunted by Clio Sara, he was adapted to the Lower-Northern climate and as such is depicted as more reptilian or behemoth-like. When going into harsh climates, he was known to take aspects of mammalian predators due to their higher tolerance to cold.
