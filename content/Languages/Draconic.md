Draconic is the language spoken by [[Dragons]]. 

It was created by [[Non-Player Characters/Urvox]], the Convoker, and was taught to dragons, as well as pre-[[Events/Agon]] humans in western [[Locations/Ethrys]]. Draconic derives from the [[Languages/Old Tongue]], and is the basis of the modern [[Yuphrali]] language.
