The Heavenly Art of Veil is a type of [[Magic/Magic]]. It is also the name of the heavenly border separating Eternus from the [[Outer Realm]], this border being where the power of Veil is sourced from. Veil was only able to be used by mortals after the demon lord [[Non-Player Characters/Orcus]] slayed [[Non-Player Characters/The Shepherd]], thus allowing access to the Veil's power and making resurrection and arcane magic possible.
