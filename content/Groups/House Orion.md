House Orion is the head of academia in the [[Sara Dominion]]. They are descended from [[Saint Orion]], who wrote the New [[Predator's Bible]].

Their family artefact is the Spear of Antares. The current head of house is Mallory Orion, who is considered the most powerful Orion in ages, being able to wield Antares with lethal efficiency.

Their symbol is a scorpion, and their words are "We are the heart".
