###### (he/him) - NPC
Xander Mentis was a human from the [[Locations/Sara Dominion]]. Nestor’s two sons, Xander and [[Non-Player Characters/Atlas Mentis]], were raised in [[Locations/Arkas]] just before [[Events/Agon#The Second Agon\|The Second Agon]]. Xander was a promising young oracle, who was overcome by visions while under the night sky. During his time as oracle, he had a recurring vision of stars falling into a gaping dark mouth. Arkas was obliterated by [[Groups/The Triad]] during Xander’s reign, killing both brothers.

Xander had visions while under the night sky. 

He was oracle from 811 GA - 0 CA (4 years).
