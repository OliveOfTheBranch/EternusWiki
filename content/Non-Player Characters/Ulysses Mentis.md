###### (he/him) - NPC
Ulysses Mentis was an elf from the [[Locations/Sara Dominion]]. He was only 15 when his father, [[Non-Player Characters/Pataras Mentis]], was killed. His aunt, [[Non-Player Characters/Rei Mentis]], was the oracle for three years before he replaced her on his 18th birthday. He held the position for a decent amount of time, and oversaw no major events. He spent much of his time near the sea, preferring his house in [[Port Kal’Vess]]. He would spend hours sitting on the rocks, watching his visions form from the sea spray. He had one daughter, [[Non-Player Characters/Sapphira Mentis]], who became oracle after her father’s retirement. After leaving his position, he spent the rest of his days sailing. 

Ulysses saw visions in sea spray, fog, and mist.

He was oracle from 148 GA - 203 GA (55 years).
