###### City
Chimaera is the capital city of the [[Locations/Sara Dominion]], and the site where the god [[Non-Player Characters/Typhon]] was slain by [[Non-Player Characters/Clio Sara]].

The city consists of districts separated by the River Dann flowing through it. Closest to the palace is the "Uptown" ward, where many nobles and upper-middle class individuals reside, and the campus of the [[Academia Chimaera]], the city's university.
Crossing the bridge over the River Dann, the Market and "Downtown" wards can be found. The Market, while largely being a trade area, is also highly residential. The Downtown ward, also known as "The Belly of the Beast", or just "The Belly" colloquially, is a poorer residential area that also contains many parlours and clubs in a raucous nightlife scene.
