###### Pantheon
The current gods of Eternus include both the remaining gods of [[Groups/The Twelve Who Came Before]] and the new usurper gods. These include:

- **[[Non-Player Characters/Lathander]], the Morninglord**
- **[[Non-Player Characters/Pölark]], the Crushing Deep**
- **[[Titania]], the Fey Queen**
<br>
- [[Asmodeus]], Lord of the Hells
- [[Clio Sara]], Goddess of the Hunt
- [[Orcus]], God of Undeath
<br>
- [[Non-Player Characters/Alexandria]], Goddess of the Moon
- [[Gruumsh]], the Ruiner
- [[Lolth]], the Spider Queen
- [[Sigurd]], God of Heroes
- [[Primus]], God of Invention
- [[The Court of Pain]]

This list is separated into three. The first three gods are original primordials, part of [[Groups/The Twelve Who Came Before]]. The next three gods ascended to their position after the First [[Agon]], which left only the Eight Who Remained. Not included in this list is [[Fafnir]], another god who ascended by killing [[Urvox]], as he himself was killed in the Second [[Agon]] by [[Sigurd]], thus making him the only usurper god to have ascended but not currently be a god. The third list is of those who ascended during the Second [[Agon]], when the most gods were killed.
