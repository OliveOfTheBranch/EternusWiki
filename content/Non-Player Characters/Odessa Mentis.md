###### (she/her) - NPC
Odessa Mentis was an elf from the [[Locations/Sara Dominion]]. Born in [[Locations/Drachma]], Odessa grew up surrounded by the hustle and bustle of city life. As a child, she would watch birds from the windows, tracking their movements, and eventually, learning to shoot them out of the sky. Unhappy with the city, she convinced her parents to move away to [[Oldegate]], where the world was still wild. It was there that she found her gift. Odessa prophesied using the entrails of [[Creatures/Chimaera (Creatures)\|Chimaera]], which she hunted and killed, becoming oracle at 34. Most of her prophecies came quickly, though some remained a mystery for hundreds of years. She saw drought, disease, and famine spread across the world. She saw the chimaera disappearing, clinging to the quiet places across the edges of the country. She foresaw the end of the [[Events/Golden Age]] and the Time of Strife. She married a fey from [[Shrike]], and had one daughter, [[Non-Player Characters/Desdemona Mentis]], beginning what was known as the “Brutalist Line”. At the age of 92, she was diagnosed with [[Magic/Spellrot]], and disappeared into the forest. Due to her condition, it was decided that her cousin would take the title after her. 

Odessa saw the future through extispicy on chimaera she had hunted.

She was oracle from 495 GA - 553 GA (58 years). 

She left her bone knife and a large collection of bone and aether jewellery in the [[Shrike's Tine]].
