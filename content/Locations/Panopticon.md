###### Orrery
The Panopticon, also known as Skypiercer, is a tower in [[Locations/Arkas]] that stretches infinitely into the sky. It was created by [[Non-Player Characters/Aureon Mentis]] before [[Events/Agon#The First Agon\|The First Agon]], and is the place that he retired to after his daughter, [[Non-Player Characters/Riparia Mentis]], was born. The Panopticon is mentioned in the [[Predator's Bible]] as the place that [[Non-Player Characters/Clio Sara]] journeyed to find the Oracle of Sara.
