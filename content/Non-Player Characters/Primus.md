###### (he/it) - NPC
The god of tinkerers and the forge, Primus resides on the plane of [[Mechanus]], hidden away in a state of constant creation. It was once an [[Athriïde]], a giant made of pure aether by [[Non-Player Characters/Moradin]] himself. When the Second [[Events/Agon]] struck, Primus, in its hubris, believed itself to be the better creator than the Allhammer, and so slayed the god and took his place. Since then, the god has become a recluse, creating in self-imposed exile while its automata march across the planes, bringing news back to the Great Gears.
