###### Metal Alloy
Dimeritium is a rare, green-blue metal created by [[Non-Player Characters/The Shepherd]]. It is primarily found in [[Locations/Pandemonium]], and has strong, anti-magic properties. Dimeritium has the unique ability to repel [[Magic/Veil]] magic, and is primarily used by mage hunters such as the [[Vëlwathe]]. 

Dreygaard are the main producers of dimeritium, as their northernmost settlement, [[Sugadinti]], is built around a Pandemonium gateway. Dimeritium mining is a dangerous endeavor, as the Shepherd’s flock roam freely around Pandemonium, and are often attracted to the sound of miners. Due to the high mortality rate of dimeritium miners, it has become a popular punishment for Dreygaardian prisoners to be sent to Sugadinti to work in the mines.
