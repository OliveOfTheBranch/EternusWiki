**(they/it) - NPC**
![[Resources/soulweaver.png|300]]

The primordial god of dreams, Karêus breathed life into the first mortals by giving them souls based upon its own [[Magic/Animus]].

On the rare occasion that it decides to take a physical form, its avatar appears as a large deformed skeleton wearing a grey cloak. In one bony hand it holds a silver lantern containing a continually burning blue flame. In the other, it wields a huge cleaver-like sword known as [[Crescent]].
