###### (she/her) - NPC
Lily Mentis was a human from the [[Locations/Sara Dominion]]. As the only child of [[Non-Player Characters/Octavian Mentis]], Lily became the oracle after her father retired. She stayed in [[Locations/Arkas]] for a short while, before moving to [[Locations/Chimaera]] for the convenience. While there, she met her cousins, [[Non-Player Characters/Cordelia Mentis]] and [[Non-Player Characters/Petyr Mentis]], who were already residing in the Mentis Estate. Plans were made for a new house to be built closer to the palace, though in the meantime, the three oracles lived together on the one property. Soon after, she, Cordelia, and Petyr were murdered by [[Non-Player Characters/Desdemona Mentis]], who then became the Oracle. 

Lily saw the future through reflections. 

She was oracle from 606 GA - 609 GA (3 years). 

She would use a small, handheld mirror to see the future when without a scrying pool. This mirror was later placed within her skull by [[Non-Player Characters/Tessera Mentis]] in order to create an oracular focus.
