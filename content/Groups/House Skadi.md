###### Noble House
House Skadi are the wardens of the North in the [[Locations/Sara Dominion]]. They are descended from [[Sankta Skadi]].

Their family artefact is the [[Collar of Orthus]]. The current acting head of house is [[Non-Player Characters/Öntýna Skadi]], who is in charge while her parents and baby sister live in [[Locations/Dreygaard#Südris\|Südris]].

House Skadi is based in [[Locations/Öndur]], after [[Player Characters/Xenevieve Mentis-Sara]] annexed it back from [[Locations/Dreygaard]]. For many years prior to that they were based in [[Locations/Chimaera]] at the Dreygaardian embassy.

Their symbol is a cerberus, and their words are "The snow covers all."
