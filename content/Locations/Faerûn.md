###### Continent
Faerûn is the eastern continent of Eternus. Western Faerûn is home to the Free Cities of the [[Sword Coast]], such as [[Waterdeep]] and [[Neverwinter]]. Meanwhile, eastern Faerûn is considered the east edge of the world, as the materiality of the continent begins to fall into the [[Feywild]] as it bleeds into the subcontinent known as [[Alfheim]].
