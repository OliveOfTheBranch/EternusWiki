###### (she/her) - NPC
Archprior Cadmus was one of the [[Groups/Hunters of Sara]], and is responsible for the spread of [[the Priory]] into the [[Locations/Sara Dominion]]. Cadmus was born in [[Locations/Felor]], but was exiled from her home after slaying the [[Elder Dragons\|Elder Dragon]], [[Thebes]]. She was also the first individual known to have performed the ritual of [[Magic/The Divine Rites#Vox Sowing\|Vox Sowing]], giving her the ability to speak the [[Languages/Old Tongue]] fluently. She also drank blood from the heart of Thebes, allowing her to talk to birds.
