###### (she/it) - NPC
Famine is one of the five children of [[Non-Player Characters/Octavian]]. 

She was born in the Lernaean region of the [[Locations/Sara Dominion]] to an unknown mother, alongside her siblings [[War]], [[Pestilence]], [[Death]], and [[Conquest]]. She was eventually imprisoned in [[Locations/Arkas]], though managed to escape when the city was destroyed by [[Groups/The Triad]] after [[Events/Agon#The Second Agon\|The Second Agon]]. During the [[Events/Time of Strife]], she drained all life from the soil around her, leaving fields and orchards barren, and subjecting the Sara Dominion, and many surrounding countries, to 100 years of famine. She was eventually imprisoned once again, this time in the lush rainforests of the [[Hesperides]], and has ceased to cause any damage.
