Tessera Mentis was an elf from the [[Locations/Sara Dominion]]. Tessera was born with no oracular power. However, due to House Mentis beginning to reclaim some of its lost status, it was decided that he would keep up appearances of having power (specifically seeing the future through vivid prophetic visions while reading) until he fathered a child capable of replacing him. Tessera went along with this plan until his son, [[Non-Player Characters/Croesus Mentis]], was born, after which he continued to stay as oracle in an attempt to stay in control of the house. Under the command of King [[Orion Sara]], he was eventually forced to pass on his title to Croesus, and retired to the Old Mentis House, where he died.
