###### Country
Yupral is the largest country in [[Locations/Ethrys]], encompassing the whole western side of the continent, from the cold, northern [[Dreaming Wood]], all the way to the southern rainforests of [[Locations/Felor]]. 

Yuphral was named after the god, [[Non-Player Characters/Yuphra]], who wove together the souls of mortals. It is home to the oldest human settlements, including the city of [[Crux]], as central Yuphral was the place where mortals were created. The early Yuphrali people spoke [[Draconic]], and worshipped Yuphra, as well as the other members of [[The Twelve That Came Before]]. 

After [[Events/Agon#The Second Agon\|The Second Agon]], when Yuphra was slain by [[Non-Player Characters/Alexandria]], the entire country underwent a massive religious upheaval. Churches and libraries were burned, as the Yuphrali people denounced Yuphra, and embraced Alexandria as their new god. This rejection of the old gods caused the [[Locations/Ashen Empire]] to form on the Ashen Islands, an area that was previously considered a part of Yuphral.
