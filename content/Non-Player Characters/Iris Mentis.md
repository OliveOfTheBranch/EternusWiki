###### (she/her) - NPC
Iris Mentis was a human from the [[Locations/Sara Dominion]]. Iris was renowned as the first Mentis to have two Southern parents, and represented her home in [[Regulus]] with pride. She was a fierce warrior, and due to being far back in the line of succession, spent much of her time sparring and training, aiming to create an Oracular Guard. Later, she moved to [[Locations/Arkas]] with her father to join the rest of the family, where she was killed by [[Groups/The Triad]] during the Fall of Arkas. 

Iris had visions come to her during the heat of combat, and she was also a proficient short-ranged oracle.
