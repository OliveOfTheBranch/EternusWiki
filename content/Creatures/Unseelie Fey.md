###### Fey
Unseelie fey are a type, or court, of [[Creatures/Fey]] that were created by or aligned themselves with [[Non-Player Characters/Mab]] as their ruler. They are typically seen as more cruel and chaotic than their [[Creatures/Seelie Fey\|Seelie]] counterparts, though in reality this isn't always the case. Unseelie fey include, but are not limited to, the following types of fey:
- [[Drow]]
- [[Shadar'kai]]
- [[Hags]]
- [[Darklings]]
- [[Redcaps]]
- [[Peatborn]]
- [[Brigganocks]]
- [[Hedgekin]]
- [[Waxers]]
- Autumn and Winter [[Eladrin]]

After [[Non-Player Characters/Mab]]'s death in the [[Events/Agon#The Second Agon\|The Second Agon]], the Unseelie Court disbanded and their capital city, [[Ravenfall\|Ravenheart]], was cast into the [[Abyss]] by [[Non-Player Characters/Lolth]]. A few unseelie [[Archfey]] went on to join the United Court of [[Locations/Feywild]] being formed by [[Non-Player Characters/Titania]], thought most chose to find their own way.
