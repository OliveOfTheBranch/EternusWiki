The Heavenly Art of Veil is a type of [[Magic/Magic]]. It is also the name of the heavenly border separating Eternus from the [[Outer Realm]], this border being where the power of Veil is sourced from. Veil was only able to be used by mortals after the demon lord [[Orcus]] slayed [[The Veilkeeper]], thus allowing access to the Veil's power and making resurrection possible.
