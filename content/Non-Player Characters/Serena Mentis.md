###### (she/her) - NPC
Serena Mentis was an elf from the [[Locations/Sara Dominion]]. Serena was born in a small coastal town, shortly before [[Events/Agon#The Second Agon\|The Second Agon]]. She married an elf, and had a son, [[Non-Player Characters/Pontus Mentis]], who was only 8 at the time of the Agon. She was unable to read the future, and as such, her mother, [[Non-Player Characters/Belladonna Mentis]], briefly became oracle after the Second Agon, ruling for 8 years in her old age, before giving the position to her son, Pontus.
