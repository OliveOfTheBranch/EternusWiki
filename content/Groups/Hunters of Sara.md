The hunters of Sara are comprised of [[Non-Player Characters/Clio Sara]] herself alongside eleven other saints. These saints are the people who aided Clio on her hunt to slay the god [[Typhon]], and who ascended to sainthood after she became a god. These people are:
- [[Aureon Mentis]]
- [[Saint Orion]]
- [[Non-Player Characters/Saint Meleager]]
- [[Saint Lerna]]
- [[Saint Erymr]]
- [[Saint Diana]]
- [[Saint Atalanta]]
- [[Sankta Skadi]]
- [[Saint Nemea]]
- [[Saint Ceryn]]
- [[Saint Cadmus]]
