###### Fey
Ols are a unique subspecies of fey, created from [[Creatures/Changelings]] that have become completely removed from society. Ols are able to change themselves in strange, uncanny ways, taking the form of whatever they like, though often existing in a twisted form that only vaguely resembles the creatures that inspired it. Once a changeling becomes an ol, it can never return to what it was before.
