###### (he/him) - NPC
Cyrus Mentis was an elf from the [[Locations/Sara Dominion]]. Cyrus’s older sister, [[Non-Player Characters/Medora Mentis]], became oracle at 29, taking over from their mother, [[Non-Player Characters/Arabella Mentis]]. They both spent much of their time in [[Locations/Drachma]], the home of their father, though occasionally visited [[Locations/Chimaera]] for business. His sister was the oracle in power during the trade of [[Groups/House Erymr]] with [[Groups/House Lerna]]. Cyrus ended up forming a good relationship with the newly changed House Lerna, using his visions to help with investments. He married a human and had a daughter, [[Non-Player Characters/Cressida Mentis]], who eventually became oracle. 

Cyrus could fall into vivid prophetic visions through reading.
