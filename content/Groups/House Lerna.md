###### Noble House
House Lerna is the head of trade in the [[Locations/Sara Dominion]]. They are descended from [[Saint Lerna]].

Their family artefact is the Shield of [[Creatures/Aegis]]. The current head of house is [[Non-Player Characters/Penelope Lerna]].

They were once the head of defence, before a trade was initiated between them and [[Groups/House Erymr]] due to House Lerna being based in [[Locations/Drachma]], the centre of trade for the Sara Dominion.

Their symbol is a hydra, and their words are "Devour their wealth."
