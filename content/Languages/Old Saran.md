Old Saran is the language spoken by [[Non-Player Characters/Typhon]] and his [[Creatures/Chimaera (Creatures)\|Chimaera]] generals, and was later adopted by the people of the Hunting Grounds. It is the origin of modern [[Saran]] and [[Dreygaardian]], and is still spoken in the [[Locations/Sara Dominion]] during important speeches and ceremonies.
