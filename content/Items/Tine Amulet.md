###### Artefact
The Tine Amulet is an artifact of [[Groups/House Mentis]]. 

It was created by [[Non-Player Characters/Cressida Mentis]] and [[Castor Meleager]] shortly after the [[Saran Civil War]]. 
The amulet allows its rightful wearer to enter the [[Locations/Shrike's Tine]] from any location, without use of the main Tine entrances. It can only be used by members of House Mentis that the Tine has already deemed worthy. 

The Tine Amulet is currently owned by Queen [[Player Characters/Xenevieve Mentis-Sara]].
