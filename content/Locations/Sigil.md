###### City
Sigil is a magical city that orbits [[Locations/Carceri]]. It is also known as the City of Doors. The city is ruled by [[Groups/The Court of Pain]].

It was once located in [[Locations/Alfheim]], but was lifted up from the ground by the Court of Pain after they gained power in the second agon. Sigil was one of the two magic cities, alongside [[Locations/Arkas]], and was targeted by [[Groups/The Triad]] after [[Events/Agon#The Second Agon\|The Second Agon]]. Fortunately, Arkas was attacked first, and Sigil had time to lift up, causing the destruction headed its way to fall upon the ground beneath it, creating [[Locations/Landfall]].
