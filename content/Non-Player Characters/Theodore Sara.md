###### (he/him) - NPC
King Theodore Sara is the current ruling monarch of the [[Locations/Sara Dominion]]. He is a human, and is also known as the "Cursèd King".

Theo has short brown hair and is fairly lithe, being a hunter.

He is married to Queen [[Player Characters/Xenevieve Sara]], and has two children by her, being [[Anastasia Sara]] and [[Victoria Sara]], with Anastasia being the heir to his throne.

Theo is in possession of [[Ladon]], a chimaera general sequestered within an artefact belonging to [[House Meleager]], which was given to him by [[Dalys Meleager]].

As a Sara, he is descended from the goddess [[Non-Player Characters/Clio Sara]], who began the royal line of [[Groups/House Sara]].
