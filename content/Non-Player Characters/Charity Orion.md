###### (she/her) - NPC
Charity Orion is the younger sister of [[Non-Player Characters/Mallory Orion]] and [[Nero Orion]]. She is also known as Charlotte or "The Charlotte".

She has a bit of a rebellious streak, and can often be found at [[Locations/The Old Faithful]]. During [[Events/Agon#The Rending\|The Rending]], Charity formed an unlikely friendship with [[Player Characters/Xenevieve Sara]], connecting two houses that have been at odds since the [[Events/Golden Age]].
