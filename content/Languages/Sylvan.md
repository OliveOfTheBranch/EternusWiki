Sylvan is the language of the [[Locations/Feywild]]. It is spoken mostly by [[Creatures/Fey]], and is the predecessor of [[Languages/Elvish]].
###### Notable Sylvan Words
- Doma - House
- Papillon - Butterfly
- Arka - Thunder
- Mord - Death
- Pyra - Fire
- Via - Voice
- Menta - Mind
- Shrike - Blade
- Silf - Fey
- Hymn - Home
###### Grammar
Most Sylvan word's show possession using an apostrophe:
- Via'Arka - Voice of Thunder

However, some irregular words are changed to their possessive form and given the preposition "de":
- House (Doma) of Butterflies = Domus de Papillons
- Mind (Menta) of Blade = Mentis de Shrike
