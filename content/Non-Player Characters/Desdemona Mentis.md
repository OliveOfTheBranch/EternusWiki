###### (she/her) - NPC
Desdemona Mentis was a elf from the [[Locations/Sara Dominion]]. She was the only daughter of [[Non-Player Characters/Odessa Mentis]], and was set to inherit her title. Due to her mother’s illness, the council were concerned that Desdemona would be a poor choice for the role, and it was given to Odessa’s cousin instead. Despite lacking the title, Desdemona was a powerful oracle, burning herbs and breathing in the smoke to see her visions. Throughout her life, she resented the fact that she’d been denied her title, and held no fondness for her country, nor her blood. When she heard that her younger relatives were all living together in [[Locations/Chimaera]], she decided to pack her things and pay a family visit. She arrived at the house, asked to stay for a night, and then sealed the exits, filling the house with poisonous gas. With no other options, the noble houses were forced to name her the Oracle of Sara. She married a half-Fey, and had three children, [[Non-Player Characters/Lorelei Mentis]], [[Non-Player Characters/Helena Mentis]], and [[Non-Player Characters/Adonis Mentis]]. 

Desdemona saw the future while breathing in smoke from herbs and incense.

She was oracle from 609 GA - 666 GA (57 years). 

As artefacts for the [[Shrike's Tine]] she left her retribution dress, an amethyst tiara, [[Non-Player Characters/Lily Mentis]]’s skull, which was later turned into an oracular focus by [[Non-Player Characters/Tessera Mentis]].
