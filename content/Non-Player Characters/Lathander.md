###### (he/him) - NPC
The primordial god of light and personification of the dawn, Lathander is the self-titled "King of the Gods". He created angels angels, and for a long time battled in a "[[Blood War]]" with [[Non-Player Characters/Pölark]], until he was betrayed by Archangel [[Non-Player Characters/Asmodeus]], whom he threw from the heavens. Now, he is a member of [[The Triad]], consisting of himself, [[Non-Player Characters/Pölark]], and [[Non-Player Characters/Titania]].

Lathander usually takes the physical form of an angel, however old religious texts often depict his literal description as that of the Sun or the morning. He is seen as a gracious god, however scornful at those who defy his almighty will.

In ancient times, Lathander was the patron god of the expanse of land now known as the [[Locations/Daymora Khaganate]]. With this piece of land being constantly under attack by titans and elementals, the people pleaded with Lathander to send them his aid. Lathander then created the [[Sun Drakes]], the strongest of which being [[Daymora Khagan]], and placed them upon the land to fight off the hordes of elementals. In modern times he is mostly worshipped in the Free Cities of the [[Sword Coast]], where religion is well removed from politics.
