###### (he/him) - NPC
Caius Mentis was a half-Elf from the [[Locations/Sara Dominion]]. He moved to the coast with his mother to relax after the war, while his older brother, [[Non-Player Characters/Octavian Mentis]], became the oracle and moved to [[Locations/Arkas]]. Caius remained in [[Locations/Port Kal'Vess]], marrying an elven woman and having two children: [[Non-Player Characters/Cordelia Mentis]] and [[Non-Player Characters/Petyr Mentis]]. Upon hearing the news of his children’s deaths, he became inconsolable in his grief, throwing himself into the sea and disappearing beneath the waves. 

Caius had vivid visions when he was afraid, and was also a proficient short-range oracle.
