###### Fiend
Devils are fiends from the [[Nine Hells]], originally created from the soil of Baator that was imbued with the rage-fuelled blood of [[Non-Player Characters/Asmodeus]]. The native language of devils is [[Infernal]].
#### Archdevils
Each layer of the Nine Hells has an archdevil as their duke or duchess. This devil may have been created as a regular devil, or turned like a fallen angel.
