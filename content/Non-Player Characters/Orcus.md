###### (he/it) - NPC
The god of cruelty and resurrection, Orcus is both a deity and a [[Demonlord]] of the [[Abyss]]. Seeking immortality during the [[Events/Agon#The First Agon\|First Agon]], Orcus fought [[Non-Player Characters/The Shepherd]] who charged itself with guarding the barrier between life and death. The fight left Orcus weakened, however, he managed to obtain the Shepherd's [[Magic/Animus]] and ascend to godhood. In doing so, the [[Magic/Veil]] was left unguarded, making arcane magic and resurrection possible, and inadvertently starting the [[Events/Golden Age]].
