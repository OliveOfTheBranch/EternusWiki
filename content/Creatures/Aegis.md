###### Chimaera General
Aegis was a hydra living in [[Locations/Loch Lerna]] (which was called Loch Aegis while it was living in it) before the formation of the [[Locations/Sara Dominion]]. It was slain by [[Saint Lerna]], who transformed it into the Shield of Aegis. The shield's visage is unsettling to look upon, except for those of Lernaean descent.
