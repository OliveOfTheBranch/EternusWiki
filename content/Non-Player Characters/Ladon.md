###### (he/it) - NPC
Ladon was a [[Creatures/Chimaera (Creatures)\|Chimaera]] general in the Hunting Grounds of East [[Locations/Ethrys]]. He mostly hunted around the Hesperides, and was defeated by [[Non-Player Characters/Saint Meleager]], who trapped him in the Orb of Ladon. 

When free from the Orb, he appeared as a large cat-like creature with menacing tusks and horns. Inside the Orb, his face presses against the metal creating the semblance of his features.

Ladon has the power to open doors to wherever he pleases, and has been utilised for ease of travel. During [[Non-Player Characters/Dalys Meleager]]'s regency, he gifted the Orb of Ladon to King [[Non-Player Characters/Theodore Sara]], who then passed it down to his daughter, Princess [[Victoria Sara]].
