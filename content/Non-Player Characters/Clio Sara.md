###### (she/her) - NPC
The goddess of predator and prey, Clio Sara was the first mortal in history to slay a god. Alongside her eleven hunters, she hunted and killed [[Non-Player Characters/Typhon]], a god who had ravaged her homeland, and in doing so ascended to godhood herself. The site of ascension became the city of [[Chimaera]], and the Hunting Grounds were renamed the [[Locations/Sara Dominion]] in her honour. She is also known as the Duchess of Wolves.

Her current heir is [[Non-Player Characters/Theodore Sara]], who is the last of her line.
