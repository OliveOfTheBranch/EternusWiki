###### (she/it) - NPC
Endelyn Moongrave was one of the three [[Hags]] that took over the domain of [[Locations/Prismeer]] after imprisoning its [[Creatures/Archfey]], [[Non-Player Characters/Tasha\|Zybilna]]. She was gifted with the powers of prophecy, and it was said that she could only be killed under the light of a full moon. 

Endelyn owned a large theater in the realm of Yon, where she enjoyed many performances, including plays, dances, and battles. While some performers were willing employees, many were prisoners, taken from all throughout [[Locations/Eternus]]. It was due to these stolen performers that Endelyn eventually met her end at the hands of the adventuring party [[Groups/Eternus Victoria]]. After their friends were kidnapped at the [[Witchlight Carnival]], Eternus Victoria made their way through Prismeer, disguising as a group of actors and performing their own rendition of [[The Gauntlet of Algon]] in order to infiltrate the theater and kill Endelyn.
