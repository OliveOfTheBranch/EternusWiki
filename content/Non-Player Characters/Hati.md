###### (he/him) - NPC
Hati is a [[Yuphral\|Yuphali]] werewolf originally from Yuphral, now living in the [[Lupercal]] and [[Locations/Chimaera]]. He was hand-picked by [[Non-Player Characters/Týr Sara]] to be the deputy of [[Groups/The Pack]]. While Týr was imprisoned by [[Non-Player Characters/Yuphra]], Hati became the de facto leader alongside [[Non-Player Characters/Sköll]].

Old magic taught to him by Týr allows him to transform into a wolf with dark grey fur. During [[Events/Agon#The Rending\|The Rending]], he became romantically involved with [[Ylga]], the current head of the Queensguard, and has since had his pack duties reduced in order to spend more time in Chimaera.
