###### (he/him) - NPC
Daenerys Fletcher is a half-elf from the [[Locations/Sara Dominion]]. He is also known as Dani.

He owns [[Locations/The Old Faithful]], a popular bar in the Belly district of [[Locations/Chimaera]]. The popularity of the bar has led to Daenerys becoming rather well acquainted with some of the younger Saran nobles. 
During [[Events/Agon#The Rending\|The Rending]], Daenerys became romantically involved with [[Non-Player Characters/Cypress of Saturnalia]], the head servant of [[Groups/House Mentis]].
