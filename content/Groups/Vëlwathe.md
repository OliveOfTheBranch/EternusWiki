###### Militia
The Vëlwathe are a branch of the [[Locations/Dreygaard\|Dreygaardian]] military tasked with hunting Vëllan. 

These expeditions to [[Locations/Vëlheim]] are incredibly dangerous, and despite the honour that it brings, few people choose to enlist. It is the art of witchcraft, an act of heresy condemned by [[Non-Player Characters/The Sanguine Lord]], that drove Dreygaard to form the Vëlwathe. Vëllan are well known for their witchcraft, making them dangerous foes, though the Vëlheim Isles are home to many other dangers, including [[Hags]], [[Vampires]], and [[Creatures/Ols]]. 

The current Warlord, [[Non-Player Characters/Magnus of Gaard]], led many expeditions into Vëlheim, and was highly regarded due to his unprecedented success rate.
