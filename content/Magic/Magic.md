Magic is a core concept and idea in Eternus, consisting of both studied, inherited, and innate powers. Magic is split into four types by arcane scholars, with these being:
- [[Magic/Cardinal]], Dark Magic
- [[Magic/Animus]], Divine Magic
- [[Magic/Veil]], Arcane Magic
- [[Magic/Primordeus]], Elemental Magic
