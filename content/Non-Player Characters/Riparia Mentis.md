Riparia Mentis was a half-fey from the [[Locations/Sara Dominion]]. The only daughter of [[Aureon Mentis]] and [[Britta of Shrike\|Brittany Mentis]]. Her father moved away to [[Locations/Arkas]] while she was young, leaving her to be raised mostly by her mother. She spent most of her time living in [[Locations/Chimaera]] and [[Oldegate]], though visited Arkas on occasion. She had a talent for scrying. She eventually married a [[Fey]], and lived out the rest of her life in Oldegate with her two children. 

Riparia saw her visions through omens of nature, such as flocks of birds and the changing of winds.

She was oracle from 82 GA - 145 GA (63 years).
