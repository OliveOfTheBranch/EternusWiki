###### Plane
Carceri is a realm, visible in the sky of [[Locations/Eternus]] Primordia as a strange black shape. It is used solely by [[Groups/The Court of Pain]], and is the location of the [[Locations/Godflow Engine]], used to store [[Non-Player Characters/The Wanderer]]'s [[Magic/Animus]]. Carceri is watched over by [[Non-Player Characters/Carter]], one of the court’s members, who serves as a warden to some of the unfortunate enemies of the court that have been held prisoner there.
