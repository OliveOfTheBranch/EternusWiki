###### City
Port Kal'Vess ([[Old Saran]]: Bay of Vess) is a coastal city in the Vess region of the [[Locations/Sara Dominion]]. It was built in the bay where the chimaera general [[Scylla]] was killed. Her death allowed the passage of ships in and out of the bay, and as such Kal'Vess was where the first human settlers of [[Locations/Alfheim]] originated from. It is named after [[House Vess]], who descend from the family of hunters who killed Scylla. 

The city is renowned for its beautiful vistas and vibrant culture, as well as being a hub of trade for the Dominion.
