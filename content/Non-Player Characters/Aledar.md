###### (she/it) - NPC
Aledar Khan was the protector of the Eastern Khanate in the [[Locations/Daymora Khaganate]], and one of the [[Creatures/Sun Drakes]] created by [[Non-Player Characters/Lathander]]. Despite being a sun drake, she also had considerable magical prowess, teaching the arts of [[Magic/Primordeus]] and eventually [[Magic/Veil]] to her people. She invented the [[Alkhani]] language, which contains elements of Veil and Primordeus such that in written form it holds magical potential. This language has since become almost extinct, save for the [[Ossindra]] who write the text in the form of tattoos on their bodies, giving them magical powers. Alkhani has become sacred to Ossindra tradition, who take Aledar Khan as their patron.
