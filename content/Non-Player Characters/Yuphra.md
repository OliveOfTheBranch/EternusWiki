###### (they/it) - NPC
The primordial god of dreams, Yuphra breathed life into the first mortals by giving them souls based upon its own [[Magic/Animus]].

It created the moon [[Locations/Arcadia]] out of a small hole in the [[Magic/Veil]], creating a plane there the glowed such that lost souls would be drawn to the light so they could pass through the hole and into the afterlife.

On the rare occasion that it decides to take a physical form, its avatar appears as a large deformed skeleton wearing a grey cloak. In one bony hand it holds a silver lantern containing a continually burning blue flame. In the other, it wields a huge cleaver-like sword known as [[Items/Weapons/Crescent]].
