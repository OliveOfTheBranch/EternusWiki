###### (she/her) - NPC
Arabella Mentis was an elf from the [[Locations/Sara Dominion]], born in [[Locations/Chimaera]]. She mostly stuck to the capital, but occasionally visited the port cities. She became oracle at 72 years old, at which point her parents moved to [[Serpent’s Folly]] to live with her uncle. She specialised in palm reading, and would frequently wander among her people, offering to tell them their future. She never foresaw any major events, but she was very popular among the people of Sara. There was some concern during her time as oracle that the [[Groups/House Mentis]] line was losing its power, though this was disproved by her children. She married an elven man from [[Locations/Drachma]], and had two children, [[Non-Player Characters/Medora Mentis]] and [[Non-Player Characters/Cyrus Mentis]]. She only served as oracle for a short time, around 38 years, before passing the title to her eldest daughter. 

Arabella saw specific futures in those whose palms she read.

She was oracle from 415 GA - 453 GA (38 years).
