###### Age
The Golden Age is the period of time spanning between the First and Second [[Events/Agon]], lasting for 814 years, and is often split into two sections. The Early Golden Age spanned from the founding of the country in 1 GA, up until the [[Saran Civil War]] in 568 GA, while the Late Golden Age occurred between 569 GA and 814 GA, when the Second Agon began, starting the [[Time of Strife]]. 

In the [[Locations/Sara Dominion]], it was a time of growth and prosperity, where the founding of arcane magic led to many scientific discoveries.
