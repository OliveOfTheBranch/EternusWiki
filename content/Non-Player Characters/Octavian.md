###### (he/they) - NPC
The primordial god of murder, Octavian the Piper was once a lonely [[Groups/Cloaked Gods\|Cloaked God]], overcome by a divine melancholy. To sate his desire for amusement, he coerced one boy to murder another playing the flute in an act of envy. The flautist became the first mortal to die, and the Piper stole his form, becoming a gleeful killer without mercy.

He was part of the [[Groups/Morteus Gods]], and was considered one of their most active members.
