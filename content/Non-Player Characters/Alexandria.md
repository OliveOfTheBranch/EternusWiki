###### (she/her) - NPC
The goddess of dreams and the Soul, nothing is remembered about Alexandria before she murdered [[Non-Player Characters/Karêus]] and took the new godly name Alexandria. As her first action as a god, she scrubbed all knowledge of her mortal self from history, leaving an immortal god in place of a mortal usurper.

The country of [[Karêal]] once revered Karêus as its patron god, however, the growing unrest within the nation and dissatisfaction at the god's refusal to accept them as its ward meant that once Alexandria rose to power and offered herself to be the Archpriestess of the nation, the people accepted her with open arms. The abandonment of [[Groups/The Twelve Who Came Before]] in Karêal incited anger in Karêal's southern dominion of isles, which ended up fracturing off to become the [[Ashen Empire]].

Alexandria almost never takes an avatar, but when she does, she appears with the same grey cloak and silver lantern that Karêus had, however missing its long, sinister blade [[Crescent]].
