###### (he/it) - NPC
Sol was the second of the [[Creatures/Sun Drakes]] to be created by [[Non-Player Characters/Lathander]], and the current khagan of the [[Locations/Daymora Khaganate]]. He became khagan after his predecessor, [[Non-Player Characters/Daymora]], was slain by an [[Elemental Titans\|Titan]]. Late in the Golden Age, he took a vicious blow from a [[Tarrasque]] that forced him to rest. Sol now only wakes every 100 years to check the status of the country, unless he is awoken by the four khans to deal with a greater threat.
