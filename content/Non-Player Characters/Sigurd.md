###### (he/him) - NPC
The god of [[Creatures/Dwarves]] and glory, Sigurd is the founder of [[Ysgard]], the eternal dwarvish heaven. During the Second [[Events/Agon]], Sigurd’s village was attacked by the dragon-god [[Fáfnir]], and Sigurd valiantly fought off the beast with nothing but a sword and his own wits. With this, he was not only honoured as a hero, but ascended to godhood. He created Ysgard within the plane of [[Acheron]] so that if ever a dwarf died a hero with a sword in their hand, they would be reincarnated to fight for peace against the legions of evil.
