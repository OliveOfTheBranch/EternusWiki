###### Noble House
House Cassalanter is one of the minor [[Groups/Saran Houses]], and is based in [[Locations/Archela]]. It also has a branch in [[Locations/Waterdeep]]. The house begun as a family of playwrights in Bas'Taurus who grew popular by basing their stories on exotic [[Creatures/Fey]] fables. The Cassalanters lived in obscurity for many generations, until a stroke of unforeseen luck caused them to become rich and popular very quickly.

The current head of house is [[Lorenzo Cassalanter]].

The head of house in Waterdeep is [[Victoro Cassalanter]].

Their symbol is the masks of comedy and tragedy.
