###### (he/him) - NPC
Aegon Sara was the son of [[Non-Player Characters/Clio Sara]] and [[Non-Player Characters/Ulysses Sara]]. He was the second ruler of the [[Locations/Sara Dominion]], taking the throne after his mother's 50 year rule. He was named after the event that freed his country, and began a trend for future monarchs crowned after an [[Events/Agon]], such as Aegon II and [[Non-Player Characters/Anastasia Sara\|Aegon III]].
