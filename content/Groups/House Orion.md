###### Noble House
House Orion is the head of academia in the [[Locations/Sara Dominion]]. They are descended from [[Saint Orion]], who wrote the New [[Predator's Bible]].

Their family artefact is the Spear of [[Antares]]. The current head of house is [[Non-Player Characters/Mallory Orion]], who is considered the most powerful Orion in ages, being able to wield Antares with lethal efficiency.

House Orion is based in [[Archela]].

Their symbol is a scorpion, and their words are "We are the heart."
