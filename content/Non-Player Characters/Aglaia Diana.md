###### (she/her) - NPC
Aglaia Diana is one of the Diana triplets, alongside [[Thalia Diana]] and [[Euthemia Diana]]. Her older brother, [[Non-Player Characters/Sirius Diana]], is the current head of [[Groups/House Diana]], and serves as the leader of the Kingsguard. Her older sister, [[Non-Player Characters/Nemorensis Diana]], is the current Master of Hounds.
