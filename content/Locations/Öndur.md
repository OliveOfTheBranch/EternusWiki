###### City
Öndur is a city in the Fenris region of [[Locations/Dreygaard]]. It was once a part of the [[Locations/Sara Dominion]], and was the home of [[Groups/House Skadi]], though eventually ended up in the hands of Dreygaard sometime during the [[Events/Golden Age]] after being annexed by the country. The main religion of the city is now [[Non-Player Characters/The Sanguine Lord]], though many still follow [[Non-Player Characters/Clio Sara\|Kleos]] (despite it being illegal) due to her prior influence there.
