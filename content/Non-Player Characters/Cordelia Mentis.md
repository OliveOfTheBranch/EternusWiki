Cordelia Mentis was an elf from the [[Locations/Sara Dominion]]. She and her brother [[Non-Player Characters/Petyr Mentis]] were raised in [[Port Kal’Vess]] by their father, [[Non-Player Characters/Caius Mentis]], but moved to their home in [[Locations/Chimaera]] once they were older. Only a few years later, their cousin, [[Non-Player Characters/Lily Mentis]], moved to Chimaera as well, and the three lived together while a new Mentis property began its construction. Cordelia was unable to see the future, but her brother Petyr was able to use leylines to do so, taking frequent trips to the [[Terrania Gorge]]. Cordelia and Petyr, as well as their cousin Lily, were all killed in the same incident involving [[Non-Player Characters/Desdemona Mentis]].
