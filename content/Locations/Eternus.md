Eternus is the name of the reality which [[Groups/The Twelve Who Came Before]] descended to, and is also commonly used as a shortening of Eternus Primordia in reference to Eternus's material plane.

Inside of Eternus, [[Magic/Primordeus]] energy writes the rules of reality, basing itself around the gods' [[Magic/Animus]]. The [[Magic/Veil]] around Eternus protects it from the [[Locations/Outer Realm]], an unspace of pure [[Magic/Cardinal]] energy where the Twelve Who Came Before and the [[Creatures/Godeater]]s come from.

In terms of Eternus Primordia, the material plane is made up of three continental landmasses: [[Locations/Ethrys]], [[Locations/Alfheim]], and [[The Pitch]]. As the divine light of [[Celestia]] shines upon Eternus Primordia, its reflection forms the [[Locations/Feywild]], while its shadow is the [[Shadowfell]].
