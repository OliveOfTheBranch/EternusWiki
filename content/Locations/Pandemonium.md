###### Plane
Pandemonium is a plane most often associated with [[Non-Player Characters/The Shepherd]]. It can be accessed through the city of [[Sugadinti]] in northern [[Locations/Dreygaard]], and is regularly visited by Dreygaardians looking to take advantage of the high concentration of [[Items/Dimeritium]] present. Pandemonium is home to the Shepherd's flock of [[Rams]], making it a rather dangerous place to visit.
