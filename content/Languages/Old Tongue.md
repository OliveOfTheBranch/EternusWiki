The Old Tongue was the first and eldest language, created by [[Non-Player Characters/Urvox]] and given to the [[Elder Dragons]]. It is the basis for most current languages, with it being closest to [[Draconic]], as the elder dragons taught the Old Tongue to the [[Chromatic Dragons]] and [[Metallic Dragons]] during the [[Convoker’s War]]. The language is unlike any other in the fact that the words themselves have magical properties. Being fluent in the language doesn't just involve understanding it, it involves being able to perfectly visualise the concept you are sharing, to the point where speaking it becomes real. Using the Old Tongue to create things in the way is often called Convoking or Shouting.
###### Notable Old Tongue Words
- Yol - Fire
- Fus - Force
- Vox - Voice
- Bex - Unlocked
- Ro - Balance
- Ur - Old / Origin / First
