###### (he/it) - NPC
Daymora Khagan was the first of the [[Creatures/Sun Drakes]] to ever be created by [[Non-Player Characters/Lathander]]. He was also the largest sun drake to have ever existed, being almost twice the size of [[Non-Player Characters/Sol]], his successor. He was slain by an [[Creatures/Elemental Titans\|Elemental Titan]].
