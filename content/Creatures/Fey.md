The word "fey" refers to a wide range of creatures created by the goddesses [[Non-Player Characters/Titania]] and [[Non-Player Characters/Mab]]. They are native to the [[Locations/Feywild]], though many have spread out to live in other places, evolving to suit their environment. 

Fey populations in [[Locations/Eternus]] Primordia all eventually lose their magic after a few generations due to being disconnected from the Feywild. Descendants of humanoid fey typically evolve into elves when left in the Material Plane, while more bestial species tend to lose their magic, as well as their greater sentience. 

Fey are split into three categories: [[Seelie Fey]], [[Unseelie Fey]], and [[Wild Fey]].
