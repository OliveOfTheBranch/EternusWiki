###### City
Rymrdann is a city located in the labyrinthine depths beneath the northwest mountains of the [[Locations/Sara Dominion]]. It has been terraformed by [[Creatures/Dwarves]] and [[Gnomes]] into a cavernous city. Dug into the side of [[Mount Dann]] is an enormous archway, the main gate into the city. The city itself is made up of twisting tunnels and caves and as such having a native guide is essential. Rymrdann exports the most stone and metals in all of [[Locations/Ethrys]], along with many gnome-made inventions. No one knows how deep the caves go, with some theories saying the tunnels are capable of going deep enough to pass planar borders.

[[Saint Erymr]] came from this region, which remains to be the home of [[Groups/House Erymr]].
