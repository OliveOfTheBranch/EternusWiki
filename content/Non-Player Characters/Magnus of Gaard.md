###### (he/him) - NPC
Magnus of Gaard is the current Warlord of [[Locations/Dreygaard]], and controls the military aspects of the country. 

He was born in Dreygaard City, and joined the [[Vëlwathe]] as a young man. He left on many expeditions to the [[Vëlheim]] Isles, and eventually began leading his own groups there due to his unprecedented success rate. Magnus became Warlord during [[Events/Agon#The Rending\|The Rending]], after the previous Warlord was removed from the position due to an unfortunate interaction with Queen [[Player Characters/Xenevieve Mentis-Sara]] and [[Groups/The Pack]] during a trip to Fenris.
