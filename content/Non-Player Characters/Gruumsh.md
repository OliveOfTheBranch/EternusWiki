###### (he/him) - NPC
The god of violence and murder, Gruumsh was an ogrelord of [[Acheron]] when he killed [[Non-Player Characters/Octavian]] in cold blood, finding himself to be a god through sheer coincidence alone. The Ruiner is known for his cruelty, having placed a curse of savagery on his legion of [[Orcs]] to give them an insatiable hunger for violence known as [[Ironlust]]. Those who are able to fight the curse renounce him as a god and flee from his iron fist.

> [!quote]
> "Gruumsh smells you, elf. You think you can kill Gruumsh? Gruumsh immortal!"
> *- The Ruiner, before he was blinded and maimed by [[Non-Player Characters/Corellon Larethian]]*

Gruumsh was slain during [[Events/Agon#The Rending\|The Rending]] during a battle on Acheron. Due to the strange difference of the Rending, his [[Magic/Animus]] did not go to his killer, but instead reformed the original owner, Octavian. After Octavian was killed by [[Non-Player Characters/Týr Sara]] and [[Player Characters/Xenevieve Mentis-Sara\|Xenevieve Mentis]], the animus returned to Gruumsh and he was revived as a god once more. During the time when he was dead, Ironlust ceased to affect the orcs under his control, causing the [[Acheronian Exodus]] as they all fled to different planes.
