###### Noble House
House Strzyga was the Royal House of [[Locations/Dreygaard]], started by [[Strzyga of Drey]] around the time of [[Events/Agon#The First Agon\|The First Agon]]. Stryzga was the lover of [[Non-Player Characters/The Sanguine Lord]], and was made into the first [[Vampires\|Vampire]]. She spread the religion of the Sanguine Lord throughout the north, uniting the people of the Northern Hunting Grounds, and founding the country that is now Dreygaard. She died in the early [[Events/Golden Age]], and her children took her place ruling the country. In 748 GA House Strzyga was ended when the Dreygaardian army staged a coup d'état and killed the Royal Family.

Before their destruction, an heir of House Strzyga sired [[Non-Player Characters/Dreyr Mentis-Strzyga]] by [[Non-Player Characters/Alexis Mentis]], who would become the first and only Oracle of Dreygaard.

Their symbol is a blood lily.
