###### (she/her) - NPC
Eldath Mentis was an elf from the [[Locations/Sara Dominion]]. Eldath, and her older brother, [[Non-Player Characters/Riparius Mentis]], gained experience by travelling the country in their youth, and learned what the people needed to escape the Time of Strife. Unfortunately, Riparius spontaneously contracted [[Magic/Spellrot]] and died young, leaving Eldath to serve as oracle in his place. Spurred on by the cause that her late brother had sought, Eldath strengthened [[Groups/House Mentis]] and brought them into the forefront of leading the country, using her gift of prophecy to end the Time of Strife in the Sara Dominion. She married an elf from [[Locations/Chimaera]] and had one son, [[Non-Player Characters/Tessera Mentis]], who had no oracular ability. Due to the state of the country, this fact was kept secret from even the other nobles.

Eldath had strong flickers of the future come to her while orating to groups of people, her visions usually being about those people or whatever she was speaking on.

She was oracle from 72 CA - 125 CA (53 years).
