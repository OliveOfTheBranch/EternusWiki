###### (she/her) - NPC
Irene Mentis was an elf from the [[Locations/Sara Dominion]]. [[Non-Player Characters/Alexis Mentis]], her older sister, became the Oracle of Sara at 18, as soon as she was old enough to take on the role, though Irene eventually replaced her due to concerns about Alexis’ suitability. Irene interpreted the future through smoke patterns, and helped to restore some faith in the future of [[Groups/House Mentis]]. She married a half-elf from [[Locations/Arkas]], moving there to get away from [[Locations/Chimaera]], and had a son, [[Non-Player Characters/Nestor Mentis]], who became the oracle after her. 

Irene prophesied using smoke patterns.

She was oracle from 720 GA -  753 GA (33 years).
