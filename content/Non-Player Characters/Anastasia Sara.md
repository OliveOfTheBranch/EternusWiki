###### (she/her) - NPC
Princess Anastasia Sara is a half-elf from the Sara Dominion. She is also known as Ana. As the eldest daughter of King Theodore Sara and Queen Xenevieve Sara, she is first in the line of succession. She lives with her parents, and her younger sister, Princess Victoria Sara, in the Royal Palace in Chimaera. 

She has long brown hair and blue eyes. Unlike her mother, Anastasia has no magical abilities.
