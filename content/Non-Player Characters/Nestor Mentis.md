Elio Mentis was a half-Elf from the [[Locations/Sara Dominion]]. Nestor was the only son of [[Non-Player Characters/Irene Mentis]], and was raised in [[Locations/Arkas]] with his parents. He learnt to read the future through the stars, and became the Oracle of Sara when he was 23 years old. He married a human woman, and had two sons, [[Non-Player Characters/Xander Mentis]], and [[Non-Player Characters/Atlas Mentis]]. Later in his life, Nestor reportedly felt as though the stars were looking back at him whenever he tried to read them, causing him to retire from his position, passing it on to his eldest son, Xander, who was 20 years old at the time. He and his sons all died during the destruction of Arkas, leaving [[Non-Player Characters/Belladonna Mentis]] as the sole heir to [[Groups/House Mentis]]. 

Nestor saw the future in the stars.

He was oracle from 753 GA - 811 GA (58 years).
