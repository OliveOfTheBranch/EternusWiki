###### (he/him) - NPC
The primordial god of the forge, Moradin was the creator of [[Creatures/Dwarves]], azer, and [[Athriïde]]s. 

He is worshipped in [[Rymrdann]], a dwarvish city in the [[Locations/Sara Dominion]], and in many other dwarvish cities across [[Locations/Eternus]]. 

During the Second Agon, he was murdered by [[Non-Player Characters/Primus]], an athriïde of his own creation who sought the power of invention and creation for itself. After his death, all athriïdes save for Primus became inert, and many were mined for the [[Items/Aether]] within their bodies.
