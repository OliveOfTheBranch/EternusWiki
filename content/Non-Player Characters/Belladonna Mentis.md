###### (she/her) - NPC
Belladonna Mentis was a half-Elf born in an unknown border town between the [[Locations/Sara Dominion]] and [[Dreygaard]]. She was considered to be last in the line of succession, due to her mother’s reputation, as well as her father’s Dreygaardian heritage. She consumed small amounts of poison, and fell into trances that allowed her to speak prophecies. She moved away from her parents as a teenager, wanting to live a quiet life by the sea. She rarely performed prophecies due to the risks associated with her divination method. She eventually married an elf from [[Port Kal’Vess]], and settled down in a small coastal town, where she had one daughter, [[Non-Player Characters/Serena Mentis]]. Her daughter was unable to read the future, and as such, Bella briefly became oracle after [[Events/Agon#The Second Agon\|The Second Agon]], ruling for 8 years in her old age, before giving the position to her grandson, [[Non-Player Characters/Pontus Mentis]]. 

Belladonna consumed poison to induce visions.

She was oracle from 0 CA - 8 CA (8 years).
