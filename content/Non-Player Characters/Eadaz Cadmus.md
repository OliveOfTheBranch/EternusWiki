###### (she/her) - NPC
Prioress Eadaz Cadmus is the current head of [[Groups/House Cadmus]]. She is also known as Ead.

When Eadaz was a young girl, she became the lone survivor of a house fire that wiped out the rest of House Cadmus, leaving her scarring burn marks across her face and body. She became close with many of the younger Saran nobles, and during [[Events/Agon#The Rending\|The Rending]], formed a special bond with King [[Non-Player Characters/Theodore Sara]] and Queen [[Player Characters/Xenevieve Mentis-Sara]]. 

Eadaz was the champion of House Cadmus during King Theodore’s coronation, and also the officiant of his wedding to Queen Xenevieve. She performed a ritual known as [[Magic/The Divine Rites#Vox Sowing\|Vox Sowing]], giving her the ability to fluently speak the [[Languages/Old Tongue]].
