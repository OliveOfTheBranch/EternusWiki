###### Era
The Time of Strife was a period of time starting from the end of [[Events/Agon#The Second Agon\|The Second Agon]], and lasting for approximately 100 years. 

In [[Locations/Ethrys]], the Time of Strife began with the destruction of the city of [[Locations/Arkas]] in the [[Locations/Sara Dominion]]. This released [[Famine]] into the world, and mass crop failure quickly took root, crippling the population. While this affected all of Ethrys, it had the largest impact on the Sara Dominion, driving them back to hunting, and drastically reducing their [[Creatures/Chimaera (Creatures)\|Chimaera]] numbers until they were no longer stable enough for commercial hunting. 

In [[Locations/Alfheim]], the Time of Strife began with the displacement of [[Locations/Sigil]], and the subsequent creation of [[Locations/Landfall]].
