###### Elder Evil
Godeaters are [[Magic/Cardinal]] beings from beyond the [[Magic/Veil]]. They are capable of devouring [[Magic/Animus]], and act animalistically in their hunger for it, in a way that is impossible for any mortal mind to fully comprehend.

The only time a godeater was able to get inside the Veil and into Eternus was during the [[Events/Rending]], when the Veil was split open, allowing it to move through. The godeater chose three avatars, being the three people with the most Cardinal surrounding them, which was [[Fenrir]], [[Non-Player Characters/Tasha]], and [[Player Characters/Xenevieve Sara]].
