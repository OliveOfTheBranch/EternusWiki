###### City
Oldegate is a city built between the western mountains of the [[Locations/Sara Dominion]] on the border with [[Yuphral]]. It was founded by [[Non-Player Characters/Britta of Shrike]], who created a [[Creatures/Fey]] gateway in the area. After Britta's death, ownership of the city was passed to [[Groups/House Atalanta]], who originated from the Oldegate region. A large portion of the population there is fey or elven, leading to a unique culture that takes from both human and fey society. 

One of the only ways to get rare magical items in the Sara Dominion is through the [[Market of Roots]] in Oldegate. The market resides in tunnels dug beneath the earth of the city held up by roots. While technically illegal, it is overlooked by House Atalanta due to its thriving trade.
