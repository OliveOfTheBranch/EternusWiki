###### Noble House
House Erymr is the head of defence in the [[Locations/Sara Dominion]]. They are descended from [[Saint Erymr]].

Their family artefact is the Cornucopia. The current head of house is [[Marissa Erymr]].

They were once the head of trade, before a trade was initiated between them and [[Groups/House Lerna]] due to House Lerna being based in [[Drachma]], the centre of trade for the Sara Dominion. House Erymr is based in [[Rymrdann]].

Their symbol is a boar, and their words are "Fortify, assess, advance."
