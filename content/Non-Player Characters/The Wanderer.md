###### (they/them\*) - NPC
The primordial god of pain, the Wanderer was the most elusive god of [[Groups/The Twelve Who Came Before]]. Not much is known about them, not even their name, except for the fact that they hold a penchant for pain, and how it twists and moulds mortals’ minds and souls. They were murdered by [[Non-Player Characters/Tasha]] during the Second [[Events/Agon]], who used it's [[Magic/Animus]] to create the [[Locations/Godflow Engine]] and form [[Groups/The Court of Pain]].

They are considered part of the [[Groups/Morteus Gods]].

\*The Wanderer's chosen gender and pronouns, much like the rest of their personality, is unknown, so worshippers default to using they/them.
