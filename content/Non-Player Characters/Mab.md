###### (she/her) - NPC
The primordial goddess of fate, Mab was queen of ravens and the Unseelie fey. The goddess [[Non-Player Characters/Titania]] was considered to be her sister, as the two were very close in their ruling over the fey people. She was revered by the Unseelie fey as the only leader to be able to have some level of control over the darker fey, such as hags and peatborn. She was murdered by Lolth, the captain of her guard, during the Second [[Events/Agon]], causing the fracturing of the Unseelie Court as no apt ruler could take her place.

As an [[Archfey]], she ruled over the domain of Ravenheart (which is now known as [[Ravenfall]]). Mab usually took the form of a pale [[Spriggan]], with jet black hair and dark butterfly wings.
