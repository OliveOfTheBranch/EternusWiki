###### (she/her) - NPC
Khaos Rosik is a [[Tiefling]] from the [[Impious Alliance]], and is the current Prime Lady of [[Neverwinter]]. She is the current head of [[House Rosik]], as daughter of [[Sol Rosik]].

She was a member of the adventuring party [[Zhetrics]].
