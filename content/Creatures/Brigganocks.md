###### Fey
Brigganocks are a species of fey native to the stormy cliffs of the Yon realm of [[Locations/Prismeer]]. 

They are rat-like in appearance, and live in large, elaborate mines. Their lifespan is short, only spanning around a week, though their stories and culture survive far longer. When the [[Rending Ritual]] was performed, all life in Prismeer ended, though a small group of Brigganocks survived the disaster by travelling alongside [[Groups/Eternus Victoria]] and escaping the realm. Though their population was once as low as perhaps 10 individuals, after being reintroduced to their old habitat, their numbers quickly rose again, and the species is now thriving. 

Prior to the Rending Ritual, a civilisation of Brigganocks within Yon was devoted to worshipping the [[Non-Player Characters/Carter of Marwin\|Grazed One]]
