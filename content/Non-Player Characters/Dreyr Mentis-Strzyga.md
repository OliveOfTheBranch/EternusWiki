###### (he/him) - NPC
Dreyr Mentis-Strzyga was a half-Elf from [[Locations/Dreygaard]]. Dreyr was the child of [[Non-Player Characters/Alexis Mentis]] by a member of the Dreygaardian royal family. He was kept secret from the Saran populace for all of his youth, being raised as a prince and being taught blood magic from a young age to hide his connection to [[Groups/House Mentis]]. Once his abilities began to manifest, it was announced to the world that Dreygaard now had their own personal oracle, causing chaos to erupt in the [[Locations/Sara Dominion]]. Not long after, however, Dreygaard staged a revolution where every member of [[Groups/House Strzyga]] was murdered, including Dreyr and Alexis.

Dreyr had vivid visions of the future while losing blood.

His time spent as the Oracle of Dreygaard is unknown.
