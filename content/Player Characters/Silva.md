###### (she/her) - Played by Mieke
Silva is a wood elf from [[Locations/Chimaera]], and member of [[Eternus Victoria]].

Silva grew up in [[Saint Meleager's Home for Children]], an orphanage in Chimaera, but left to explore the world as soon as she was 18. Her parents died in the [[Battle of Oldegate]]. Her brother, who she met at the orphanage, is actually a changeling by the name of [[Non-Player Characters/Aryas]]. Growing up, Aryas grew attached enough to Silva for his stable form to manifest as a more masculine version of Silva herself. When Silva left the orphanage, she left Aryas behind.

While travelling, she found an abandoned dragon egg in the mountains of eastern [[Felor]]. She hatched a baby [[Elder Dragons\|Elder Dragon]] from the egg, the first of its kind in centuries, and called it Mushroom. After [[Events/Agon#The Rending\|The Rending]], Mushroom's growth rate increased exponentially, and he was soon large enough for her to ride on.
