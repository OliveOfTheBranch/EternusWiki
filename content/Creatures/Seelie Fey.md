###### Fey
Seelie fey are a type, or court, of [[Creatures/Fey]] that were created by or align themselves with [[Non-Player Characters/Titania]] as their ruler. They are typically seen as more benevolent and tricksy than their [[Creatures/Unseelie Fey\|Unseelie]] counterparts, though in reality this isn't always the case. Seelie fey include, but are not limited to, the following types of fey:
- [[Faeries]]
- [[Pixies]]
- [[Sprites]]
- [[Blink Dogs]]
- [[Dryads]]
- [[Unicorns]]
- Summer and Spring [[Eladrin]]

After [[Non-Player Characters/Mab]]'s death in the [[Events/Agon#The Second Agon\|The Second Agon]], the Unseelie Court disbanded and the Seelie Court became the main court of the [[Locations/Feywild]]. In an attempt to unite the remaining unseelie, Titania formed the United Court of Feywild that consisted of both unseelie and seelie [[Archfey]]. Despite this, many unseelie claimed that they would only ever bow to Mab as their leader, and became nomadic or [[Wild Fey]], fleeing to the far reaches of the Feywild where they couldn't be disturbed.
