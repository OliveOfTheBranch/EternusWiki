###### (he/him) - NPC
Altair Mentis was an elf from the [[Locations/Sara Dominion]]. He was raised by his mother, [[Non-Player Characters/Sapphira Mentis]], in [[Locations/Port Kal'Vess]]. When he was just a baby, his older half-brother, [[Non-Player Characters/Lysander Mentis]], became the Oracle of Sara, moving away to [[Locations/Chimaera]], and visiting very rarely. When he was 15, his parents died at sea, leaving him to move in with Lysander. Despite their difference in age, the two brothers got along rather well, and maintained a good relationship throughout their lives. Lysander eventually retired, moving back to [[Serpent’s Folly]] and leaving Altair to inherit his title. Altair married an elven woman, and had one daughter, [[Non-Player Characters/Arabella Mentis]]. His most notable prophecy was of a flying city, which is now believed to be [[Sigil]]. 

Altair saw the future in his dreams.

He was oracle from 335 GA - 415 GA (80 years).
