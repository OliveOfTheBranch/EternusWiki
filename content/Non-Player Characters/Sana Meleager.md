###### (she/her) - NPC
Sana Meleager is the current head of [[Groups/House Meleager]]. 

Her older brother, [[Non-Player Characters/Dalys Meleager]] was the previous head of House Meleager, and served as Regent of the [[Locations/Sara Dominion]] for 18 years. During this time, Sana lived in [[Locations/Cassowary]]. She is a mage, and is a high-ranking member of the [[Tears of Eurymede]].

Sana was the champion of House Meleager during King [[Non-Player Characters/Theodore Sara]]'s coronation.
