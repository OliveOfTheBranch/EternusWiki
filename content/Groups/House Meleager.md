###### Noble House
House Meleager are the royal advisors and house of smiths in the [[Locations/Sara Dominion]]. They are descended from [[Non-Player Characters/Saint Meleager]], who created the Orb of [[Ladon]].

Their family artefacts are the Orb and Mask of [[Ladon]]. The current head of house is [[Sana Meleager]], who recently took up the mantle after [[Dalys Meleager]] stepped down.

House Meleager is based in [[Cassowary]].

Their symbol is a cassowary, and their words are "Do not bite the hand that feeds."
