###### Sword
Sunray is a shortsword once belonging to [[Non-Player Characters/Pataras Mentis]]. While few descriptions of the sword have survived to the [[Current Age]], we know that it was gold handled, and had some sort of fire related enchantment, though magic at this time was still in its early stages.

When Pataras died in 145 GA, Sunray was stored in the [[Locations/Shrike's Tine]] by his sister, [[Non-Player Characters/Rei Mentis]]. [[Non-Player Characters/Adonis Mentis]] later adopted the sword, taking it with him to [[Locations/Regulus]], where it was passed down to his son, [[Non-Player Characters/Elio Mentis]], and later, [[Non-Player Characters/Iris Mentis]]. It was briefly considered lost after [[Events/Agon#The Second Agon\|The Second Agon]], though was eventually located in the Shrike's Tine.
