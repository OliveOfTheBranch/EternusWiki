###### Plane
Pandemonium is a plane most often associated with [[Non-Player Characters/The Shepherd]]. It is often described as a hellscape of rocky tunnels and howling wind, so loud that it is almost impossible to hear someone talking next to you. Pandemonium is also home to a variety of monstrosities, including the Shepherd's flock of [[Rams]], making it an incredibly dangerous place to visit.

Pandemonium can be accessed via a natural phenomenon through the city of [[Sugadinti]] in northern [[Locations/Dreygaard]], and is regularly visited by Dreygaardians looking to take advantage of the high concentration of [[Items/Dimeritium]] present.
