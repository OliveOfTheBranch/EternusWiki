###### Noble House
House Sara are the royal house of the [[Locations/Sara Dominion]]. The first of the line was [[Non-Player Characters/Clio Sara]], who upon slaying the god [[Non-Player Characters/Typhon]] ("The Predator") ascended to become a goddess herself. A fragment of her power is said to run through their veins. 

Their family artefact is the Bone Crown, which was originally formed from Typhon's bones, and has since been added to by each monarch. The current ruling king and head of house is [[Non-Player Characters/Theodore Sara]]. 

House Sara is based in [[Locations/Chimaera]].

Their symbol is a bow and arrow, and their words are "Blessed is the Hunt."
