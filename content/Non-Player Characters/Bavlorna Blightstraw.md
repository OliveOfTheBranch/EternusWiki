###### (she/it) - NPC
Bavlorna Blightstraw was one of the three [[Hags]] that took over the realm of [[Locations/Prismeer]] after imprisoning its [[Creatures/Archfey]], [[Non-Player Characters/Tasha\|Zybilna]]. Bavlorna seized the swampy region, [[Locations/Prismeer\|Hither]], as her own, taking up residence near the town of Downfall, before she was eventually killed by the adventuring party [[Groups/Eternus Victoria]].
