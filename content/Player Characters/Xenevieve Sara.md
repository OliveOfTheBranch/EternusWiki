###### (she/her) - Played by Zae
Queen Xenevieve Sara (née [[Groups/House Mentis\|Mentis]]) is the current ruling queen consort of the [[Locations/Sara Dominion]]. She is a high elf, and is also known as Vivi, or the "[[Magic/Cardinal]] Queen".

Vivi has long blond ringlets. Her right eye is missing, a dark gaping hole. She can be somewhat intimidating to be in a room with, though is joyful when surrounded by those she trusts.

She is married to King [[Non-Player Characters/Theodore Sara]], and has five children by him, being [[Anastasia Sara]], [[Victoria Sara]], [[Apollo Sara]], [[Artemis Sara]], and [[Python Sara]]. Not long after Xenevieve was born into House Mentis in [[Chimaera]], her [[Pythia Mentis\|mother]] passed away of an unexplainable illness. Not long after, into her teens, her [[Croesus Mentis\|father]] died of [[Spellrot\|spellrot]]. Running away from home, she joined an adventuring party known as [[Eternus Victoria]], which led her into the [[Feywild]] in search of her lost friends. Upon returning to Chimaera, she married King Theo and ascended to the throne. 

As a [[Groups/House Mentis\|Mentis]], she is descended from [[Aureon]], the Oracle of Sara.
