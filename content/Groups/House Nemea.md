###### Noble House
House Nemea are the wardens of the South in the [[Locations/Sara Dominion]]. They are descended from [[Saint Nemea]], who was key in uniting the Southern kingdoms to join the North during the founding of the Dominion.

Their family artefact is the Cloak of [[Regulus]]. The current head of house is [[Non-Player Characters/Austra Nemea]], who is the eldest of the current head of houses.

House Nemea is based in [[Regulus]].

Their symbol is a lion, and their words are "Have pride in the South."
