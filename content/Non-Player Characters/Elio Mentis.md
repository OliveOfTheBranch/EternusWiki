###### (he/him) - NPC
Elio Mentis was a half-Elf from the [[Locations/Sara Dominion]]. As the son of [[Non-Player Characters/Adonis Mentis]], Elio lived with a dark cloud over his name. He used patterns of sunlight to tell the future, and had his home built with that in mind. He eventually married a human, and had one daughter, [[Non-Player Characters/Iris Mentis]]. Throughout his life, Elio maintained a good relationship with his cousin, [[Non-Player Characters/Irene Mentis]], and frequently visited her in [[Locations/Arkas]]. He had hoped that the two of them might be able to restore the [[Groups/House Mentis]] name, and move away from the previous generation of oracles. In his old age, he decided to permanently move to Arkas with his family. He advised [[Non-Player Characters/Nestor Mentis]] and his son, up until his death during the destruction of Arkas. 

Elio told the future using the patterns of sunlight that would dance across the floor.

He left a shard of a [[Creatures/Sun Drakes\|Sun Drake]]'s tooth that glimmers with sunlight as an artefact, still kept within his house in [[Regulus]].
