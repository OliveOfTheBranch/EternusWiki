Medora Mentis was an elf from the [[Locations/Sara Dominion]]. She became oracle at 29, taking over from her mother, [[Non-Player Characters/Arabella Mentis]]. She and her younger brother, [[Non-Player Characters/Cyrus Mentis]], spent much of their time in [[Drachma]], the home of their father, though occasionally visited [[Locations/Chimaera]] for business. She was the oracle in power during the trade of [[Groups/House Erymr]] with [[Groups/House Lerna]], and saw many visions in the stars of their two constellations. Medora married an elf and had a daughter, [[Non-Player Characters/Odessa Mentis]], who became oracle after her. 

Medora told the future from the alignments of stars. 

She was oracle from 453 GA - 495 GA (42 years).
