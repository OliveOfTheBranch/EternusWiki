###### (he/him) - NPC
Sirius Diana is the current head of [[Groups/House Diana]]. 

He is the head of the Kingsguard, and spent many years acting as King [[Non-Player Characters/Theodore Sara]]'s personal guard, all throughout the King's youth. He assisted Queen [[Player Characters/Xenevieve Mentis-Sara]] and [[Non-Player Characters/Marissa Erymr]] in their efforts to prepare King Theodore for the throne during [[Events/Agon#The Rending\|The Rending]]. 

His older sister, [[Non-Player Characters/Nemorensis Diana]], is the current Master of Hounds, and he has three younger sisters, [[Thalia Diana]], [[Euthemia Diana]], and [[Aglaia Diana]].

Sirius was the champion of House Diana during King Theodore's coronation.
