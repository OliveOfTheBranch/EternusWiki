###### (he/him) - Played by Dylan
Vuris Sanavaloth is a primordial triton originally from the oceans beneath [[The Pitch]], now living in [[Locations/Chimaera]]. He was once a devout follower of [[Aureon Mentis]], but after his god betrayed him he switched alliances to worship [[Non-Player Characters/Asmodeus]], the Lord of the Hells. He wields the [[Magic/Cardinal]] longsword [[Snark]], and is a member of [[Eternus Victoria]].
