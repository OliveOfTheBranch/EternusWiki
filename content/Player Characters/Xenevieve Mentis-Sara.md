###### (she/her) - Played by Zae
Queen Xenevieve Sara (née [[Groups/House Mentis\|Mentis]]) is the current ruling queen consort of the [[Locations/Sara Dominion]]. She is a high elf, and is also known as Vivi, or the "[[Magic/Cardinal]] Queen".

Vivi has long blond ringlets. Her right eye is missing, a dark gaping hole. She can be somewhat intimidating to be in a room with, though is joyful when surrounded by those she trusts.

She is married to King [[Non-Player Characters/Theodore Sara]], and has two children by him, being [[Non-Player Characters/Anastasia Sara]] and [[Victoria Sara]]. Not long after Xenevieve was born into House Mentis in [[Locations/Chimaera]], her [[Pythia Mentis\|mother]] passed away of an unexplainable illness. Not long after, into her teens, her [[Non-Player Characters/Croesus Mentis\|father]] died of [[Magic/Spellrot]]. Running away from home, she joined an adventuring party known as [[Groups/Eternus Victoria]], which led her into the [[Locations/Feywild]] in search of her lost friends. Upon returning to Chimaera, she married King Theo and ascended to the throne. 

Being both queen and from a noble house, Vivi speaks many languages, including [[Languages/Old Saran]], [[Languages/Elvish]], [[Languages/Sylvan]], and Saran Sign Language.

As a [[Groups/House Mentis\|Mentis]], she is descended from [[Non-Player Characters/Aureon Mentis\|Aureon]], the Oracle of Sara.
