###### Forest
The Dreaming Wood is a sparse woodland in the northernmost region of [[Locations/Yuphral]]. 

While still technically classed as a woodland, the trees in the area are all dead, and no life exists beyond the edges of the wood. It is a place closely linked with death, due to both its connection with [[Locations/Arcadia]], as well as the rumors that [[Death]] herself was imprisoned there. 

For those of the [[Non-Player Characters/Alexandria\|Alexandrian]] faith, the Dreaming Wood is a place of legend, where worthy worshippers may go to achieve enlightenment.
