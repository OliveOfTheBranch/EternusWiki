Cressia Mentis was a half-Elf from the [[Locations/Sara Dominion]]. Daughter to [[Non-Player Characters/Cyrus Mentis]], and cousin to [[Non-Player Characters/Odessa Mentis]]. She was 84 years old when she took her cousins title, and already had two sons at the time, [[Non-Player Characters/Octavian Mentis]] and [[Non-Player Characters/Caius Mentis]]. During her time as head of house, she oversaw the civil war between [[Groups/House Orion]] and [[Groups/House Meleager]], predicting and assisting in the creation of the [[Mask of Ladon]]. Waging her influence over the war’s outcome, she convinced the House of Smiths to create the [[Tine Amulet]] in secrecy. She told the future through art, painting depictions of war in the time leading up to the conflict. She retired shortly after the war ended, leaving her eldest son, Octavian, to take her place. 

It is thought that Cressida may have been a worshipper of [[Non-Player Characters/Octavian\|The Piper]], due to the naming of her son and the political landscape surrounding the time of his birth, though this may be speculation.

Cressida saw the future in her paintings.

She was oracle from 553 GA - 572 GA (19 years).
