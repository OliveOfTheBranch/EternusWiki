###### City
Drachma is a city in the Lernaean region of the [[Locations/Sara Dominion]]. It sits on the edge of [[Locations/Loch Lerna]], and is a bustling city of trade due to being placed in the crossroads of [[Locations/Chimaera]] to the North, [[Oldegate]] to the West, [[Port Kal’Vess]] to the East, and Bas'Taurus to the South.

It is the patron city of [[Groups/House Lerna]], whom the region was named after. Unlike other cities in the Sara Dominion, however, Drachma is not ruled by House Lerna. Instead, Drachma is controlled by an influential merchant council, on which the head of house has a seat.
