Helena Mentis was an elf from the [[Locations/Sara Dominion]]. After taking her position by force, [[Non-Player Characters/Desdemona Mentis]] had three children: [[Non-Player Characters/Lorelei Mentis]], Helena, and [[Non-Player Characters/Adonis Mentis]]. Lorelei heard whispers of the future whenever the world was silent, Helena could tell the future using a set of carved bones, and Adonis saw visions flickering in fire. At first, when her older sister retired, Helena declined the position of oracle, leaving her younger brother, Adonis, to inherit the position. She two daughters, [[Non-Player Characters/Alexis Mentis]] and [[Non-Player Characters/Irene Mentis]]. After starting the [[Great Fire of Chimaera]], Adonis was removed from the position, and Helena was ultimately forced to take on her brother’s role, serving as oracle until her eldest daughter, Alexis, was old enough to take over. 

Helena told the future using a set of carved bones, which she left as an artefact in the [[Shrike's Tine]] after her death. 

She was oracle from 688 GA - 698 GA (10 years).
