###### (she/it) - NPC
Siris Khan was the protector of the Southern Khanate in the [[Locations/Daymora Khaganate]], and one of the [[Creatures/Sun Drakes]] created by [[Non-Player Characters/Lathander]]. Her serpentine body was covered in fish-like scales that refracted light, causing her to look iridescent. It spent most of its time in the ocean south of the Deathreach.

Siris was considered the psychopomp of Daymoran religion, using rainbows to guide souls to the fields of [[Celestia]]. She was slain by a leviathan.
