###### Noble House
House Delphi is one of the Minor [[Groups/Saran Houses]], and is based in [[Locations/Chimaera]]. 

The current head of House Delphi is [[Markos Delphi]], though due to his criminal status, and lack of children, the House's fate is somewhat uncertain. The only other member of House Delphi is Markos's niece, Queen [[Player Characters/Xenevieve Mentis-Sara]], daughter of [[Pythia Delphi]], though Xenevieve will not be taking on the responsibilities of the house due to already being the Head of [[Groups/House Mentis]]. There is some thought that the title may potentially resurface in one of her children, though otherwise, the house will cease to be. 

Interestingly, House Delphi is known to have had several oracles, including Markos Delphi and Pythia Delphi, and it was theorised by some that the house may have split from House Mentis, though this remains unproven, and rather unlikely.
