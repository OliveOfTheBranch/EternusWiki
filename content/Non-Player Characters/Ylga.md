###### (he/him) - NPC
Ylga is a human from the [[Locations/Sara Dominion]]. 

He was born in a Saran town near the Dreygaardian border, and had a Saran mother and a Dreygaardian father. As a young man, he moved to [[Locations/Dreygaard]] City, where he joined the military and was eventually stationed in [[Locations/Dreygaard#Fenris\|Fenris]]. 

It was in Fenris, during [[Events/Agon#The Rending\|The Rending]], that Ylga was assigned to tend to Queen [[Player Characters/Xenevieve Mentis-Sara]], who was still a young noblewoman at the time, and had journeyed to the city with a pack of wolves. When [[Groups/The Pack]] left Fenris, Ylga chose to accompany them, swearing his loyalty to Xenevieve. He soon became romantically involved with [[Non-Player Characters/Hati]], one of [[Non-Player Characters/Týr Sara]]'s generals, and became an honorary member of the pack, alongside Xenevieve. 

During the Rending, he moved to [[Locations/Chimaera]] and took up residence at the [[Groups/House Mentis]] estate, serving as Xenevieve's guard, and eventually being named head of the Queensguard after Xenevieve's marriage to King [[Non-Player Characters/Theodore Sara]].
