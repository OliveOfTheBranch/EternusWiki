###### (she/her) - NPC
Sapphira Mentis was an elf from the [[Locations/Sara Dominion]]. She spent her youth living in [[Port Kal’Vess]] with her father, all the way until his retirement. She became oracle at 36, and never truly found a place to settle down. She inherited her father’s sense of adventure, and spent periods of time all over the country, though she preferred a warmer climate, and only visited [[Locations/Chimaera]] when necessary for her work. She married a human man in [[Locations/Serpent's Folly]], and had a son, [[Non-Player Characters/Lysander Mentis]]. After her husband’s unexpected death, she left the South, bringing her son to live with her in Kal’Vess, where she remarried and had a second son, [[Non-Player Characters/Altair Mentis]], with an elf from [[Locations/Alfheim]]. Due to her fairly mobile lifestyle, and disinterest in politics, she was advised to retire, and her first son was made oracle at 20 years of age. Sapphira continued raising her youngest in Kal’Vess, though unfortunately she and her husband died in a shipwreck when he was 15, leaving him to move in with his older half-brother. 

Sapphira heard whispers on the wind that told her the future. 

She was oracle from 203 GA - 263 GA (60 years). 

She left a notable sapphire jewellery collection in her wake. Though much of it has been used for outfits and decorative purposes, many are still placed on display in the Mentis Estate and the [[Shrike's Tine]].
