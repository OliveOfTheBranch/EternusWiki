###### Religion
The Priory is a religion that originated in [[Locations/Felor]], and was spread to the [[Locations/Sara Dominion]] by [[Non-Player Characters/Archprior Cadmus]]. It began as a combination of worship to [[Non-Player Characters/Urvox]] and [[Non-Player Characters/Typhon]], but has since grown to include [[Non-Player Characters/Clio Sara\|Kleos]] in its ideology. The Priory worship the concept of the hunt more so than a god itself, and in modern times have grown this ideology to encompass the idea of hunting for a better position in one's life, leading to them becoming popular among the common people.

In the early days of the Priory, many of the members were dragon hunters. In modern times, however, due to the lack of [[Elder Dragons]] and the sanctity of [[Dragons]] to the Felorian people, this purpose has been changed to instead revering dragons in their perfection of the Convoker's will.

The Priory is led by a [[Magic/The Divine Rites#Vox Sowing\|Vox Sown]] prior or prioress, who is usually the head of [[Groups/House Cadmus]]. The current prioress is [[Non-Player Characters/Eadaz Cadmus]].
