###### Primordeus Entity
Elemental titans appear when [[Magic/Primordeus]] leylines converge and form an incarnation of pure elemental energy. This often happens in places most affected by leylines, in arid climates such as the [[Locations/Daymora Khaganate]]. These titans will often appear as a high concentration of earth, water, air, or fire energy. Recorded titans have been creatures known as:
- [[Tarrasque]]
- [[Karkinos]]
- [[Ubtao]]
- Leviathan
- Phoenix
- Tempest
- Zaratan
