###### (she/her) - NPC
Princess Victoria Sara is a half-elf from the [[Locations/Sara Dominion]]. 

She is the youngest daughter of Queen [[Player Characters/Xenevieve Mentis-Sara]], and King [[Non-Player Characters/Theodore Sara]], and the younger sister of Princess [[Non-Player Characters/Anastasia Sara]]. She lives in the Royal Palace in [[Locations/Chimaera]], but is known to take frequent trips to [[Locations/Oldegate]] and [[Locations/Port Kal'Vess]] with her family. 

Victoria has wavy brown hair and hazel eyes. It is unknown whether she possesses any magical abilities.
