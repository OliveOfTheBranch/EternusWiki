###### Country
The Daymora Khaganate is a country on the southernmost point of [[Locations/Ethrys]]. It is comprised of four khanates, with their capital cities being:
- [[Northwatch]], Northern Khanate
- [[Karkinos]], Southern Khanate
- [[Locations/Alkhan and Edaros]], Eastern Khanate
- XXX, Western Khanate
### Populus
The Daymora Khaganate is split into three classes. The highest class exclusively held by the most powerful sun drake is that of Khagan. This was originally held by [[Daymora]], whom the country was named after, yet after his death the title shifted to [[Sol]], who has been khagan ever since. Below the khagan serve the Khans, which have been both drake and human. Khans are rulers over their respective areas, rather than the nation as a whole. Heirs to the title of khan, or designated lords of their khanate, are given the title Khanum. Khanums have significant sway in court, but not the authoritative power of khans. Finally, the lower class are known as [[Ossi]]. While the higher classes can ascend up the rankings to become more powerful, it is Daymoran belief that being born as an ossi is immutable. Ossi who reject this notion, wishing to seek more power, must do so through trials of combat, covering themselves in magical tattoos that brand them as “[[Ossindra]]”.
