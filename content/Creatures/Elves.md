Elves are the descendents of [[Creatures/Fey]] who chose to leave behind their home in the [[Locations/Feywild]] and settle in the Material Plane. Separated from their ancestral homeland, elves have adapted to life in [[Locations/Eternus]] Primordia, and the reduced magic that it entails. They look almost human, though often possess sharper, more angular features, and pointed ears that betray their fae ancestry. They have longer lifespans than humans, living up to as much as 750 years if they survive long enough to die of old age, though many succumb to illness or injury before they can reach that point.
