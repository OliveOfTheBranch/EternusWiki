Dreygaardian is the language spoken by people from [[Locations/Dreygaard]]. 

It is derived from [[Languages/Old Saran]], the language spoken by [[Non-Player Characters/Typhon]] and his [[Creatures/Chimaera (Creatures)\|Chimaera]] generals, and has many similarities with modern [[Saran]]. The Dreygaardian language is considered to be the youngest language in [[Locations/Ethrys]], having only become distinct from Saran around the early [[Events/Golden Age]].
