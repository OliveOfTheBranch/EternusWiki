###### Country
Felor is a country in the southwest of [[Locations/Ethrys]]. It shares borders with [[Yuphral]] and the [[Locations/Daymora Khaganate]], and is technically considered to be a part of Yuphral, though it has its own distinct culture. Felorians are a nomadic people, who share the warm plains of their homeland with the native dragon population.
