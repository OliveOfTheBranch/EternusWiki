###### Artefact
The Spear of Antares is the artefact of [[Groups/House Orion]]. 

It is a spear created by [[Saint Orion]] shortly before [[Events/Agon#The First Agon\|The First Agon]], and was made from the tail of the [[Locations/Chimaera]] general, [[Creatures/Antares]]. The spear carries a part of Antares itself, including its powers of mind distortion. This includes memory manipulation, and mind control, and can affect the whole of the [[Locations/Sara Dominion\|Saran]] population when wielded by powerful individuals. 

Interestingly, it is the Spear of Antares that selects the head of House Orion, rather than the typical rules of succession that other houses follow. 

The Spear of Antares is currently owned by [[Non-Player Characters/Mallory Orion]].
