###### Pantheon
The Twelve Who Came Before are the original gods of [[Eternus]] who descended from the heavenly [[Magic/Veil]]. The pantheon consists of:

- **[[Non-Player Characters/Lathander]], the Morninglord**
- **[[Non-Player Characters/Pölark]], the Crushing Deep**
- **[[Non-Player Characters/Titania]], the Fey Queen**
- [[Mab]], the Duskmatron
- [[Octavian]], the Piper
- [[Non-Player Characters/Typhon]], the Predator
- [[Moradin]], the Allhammer
- [[Urvox]], the Convoker
- [[Kareus]], the Soulweaver
- [[Non-Player Characters/The Sanguine Lord]]
- [[The Veilkeeper]]
- [[The Wanderer]]

With only those in bold being still alive. The rest were killed in an [[Events/Agon]], when they became mortal and their divine [[Magic/Animus]], their soul and source of their godly power, was taken from them.

The Twelve Who Came Before are now very rarely worshipped as a pantheon in and of themselves, even as many of their gods are still individually worshipped. They are considered the only officially recognised pantheon in the [[Ashen Empire]], however, as the power of the old gods waned, even this state has opted for worshipping the only living members of the Twelve, who call themselves [[The Triad]].
