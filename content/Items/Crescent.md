###### Cardinal Armament
Crescent is a sword that was created by [[Non-Player Characters/Yuphra]] to be their weapon in battle, alongside their hallowed lantern. When used by Yuphra, it was made of a silver so pure that it seemed to glow with moonlight.

When Yuphra imprisoned [[Non-Player Characters/Týr Sara]] in chains for committing the sin of changing his own soul into [[Magic/Animus]] (via [[Magic/The Divine Rites#The Wolf's Ritual\|The Wolf's Ritual]]), they stabbed him in the head with Crescent. After Týr was freed by the [[Creatures/Godeater]], the [[Magic/Cardinal]] magic corroded the blade, tarnishing it so black and dark that it no longer glowed.

The sword is still in the possession of Týr Sara.
