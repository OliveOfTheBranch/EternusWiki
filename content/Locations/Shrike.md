###### Fey Domain
Shrike is a realm in the [[Locations/Feywild]]. The people of Shrike are [[Wild Fey]], and are known for their stark white hair and striking red eyes. They are well versed in combat, and choose their [[Archfey]] via dueling. Anyone that wishes to rule may challenge the current Archfey for their title, and fight to the death. This has led to a system of government that is in a state of constant change. 

Despite the changeable nature of the realm, it has maintained a close relationship with the [[Locations/Sara Dominion]] ever since the Archfey, [[Non-Player Characters/Britta of Shrike]], created the fey crossing between Shrike and [[Locations/Oldegate]] in the early [[Events/Golden Age]]. 

The descendents of the various Shrike Archfey are joined together by [[Groups/La Domus de Papillons]], a noble [[Groups/Saran Houses\|Saran House]] in Oldegate. Despite belonging to different bloodlines, these fey are considered family under Shrike tradition.
