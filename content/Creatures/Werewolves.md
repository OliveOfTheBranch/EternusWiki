###### Blood Curse
The word werewolf refers to a specific type of lycanthrope possessing wolf or canine features, though is often used more broadly to describe lycanthropes in general. 

The first werewolf was [[Non-Player Characters/Týr Sara]], who received a blood curse from [[Non-Player Characters/Yuphra]] as part of his punishment for performing [[Magic/The Divine Rites#The Wolf's Ritual\|The Wolf's Ritual]]. This curse created lycanthropy, an incurable disease that causes a person to become wild and animalistic during the full moon. It allows the afflicted individual to take on physical and mental characteristics of a specific animal, though the selection of said animal is somewhat at random, and requires further study to understand. Lycanthropy is transferred through blood or saliva, but is only infectious when the afflicted individual is transformed. This is due to its nature of being a blood curse.

Lycanthropes tend to prefer living in groups commonly referred to as packs. These packs are formed between groups of lycanthropes, and provide structure, community, and protection. They are led by experienced pack leaders, and usually operate far from civilisation, especially in the [[Locations/Dreygaard#Lupercal\|Lupercal]], and throughout the [[Locations/Oldegate]] Forest.
