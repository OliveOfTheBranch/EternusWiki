Meleager is one of the [[Groups/Hunters of Sara]] and a saint of [[Chimaera]].

Specifically what Meleager looks like has been lost to time, but he has been seen wearing a crimson robe while on the hunt. In the [[Predator's Bible]], Meleager is described as a powerful yet headstrong hunter who suffers from hubris. Before the first [[Events/Agon]], he joined the hunters of Sara to slay the [[Non-Player Characters/Typhon\|Predator]]. Once [[Non-Player Characters/Clio Sara]] ascended to godhood, she made him immortal as well as the rest of the hunters.
