###### (he/him) - NPC
Lysander Mentis was a half-Elf from the [[Locations/Sara Dominion]]. He became oracle at 20 years of age after his mother was advised to step down by the other noble houses. When he was 33, his mother and stepfather died at sea, leaving him to care for his younger half-brother, [[Non-Player Characters/Altair Mentis]], who was only 15 at the time. They both lived in [[Locations/Chimaera]], working hard to reconnect with the other [[Groups/Saran Houses]] after two generations of travelling oracles. Despite their difference in age, the two brothers got along rather well, and maintained a good relationship throughout their lives. Lysander eventually retired, moving back to [[Serpent’s Folly]] and leaving Altair to inherit his title. 

Lysander created songs of the future. 

He was oracle from 263 GA - 335 GA (72 years).
