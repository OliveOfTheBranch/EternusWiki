###### (he/him) - NPC
Pontus Mentis was an elf from the [[Locations/Sara Dominion]]. He took over after his grandmother’s retirement when he was 16, the youngest oracle yet. He was the Oracle of Sara during the beginning of the Time of Strife, and used the clouds to tell the future. He was born in [[Locations/Port Kal'Vess]], though moved to [[Locations/Chimaera]] after becoming oracle. Pontus was a form of solace for the Sara Dominion, using his prophecies to guide the country away from the Time of Strife and into the new age. He married an elven woman from Chimaera and had two children, [[Non-Player Characters/Riparius Mentis]] and [[Non-Player Characters/Eldath Mentis]].

Pontus saw his visions by watching the clouds. 

He was oracle from 8 CA - 72 CA (64 years).
