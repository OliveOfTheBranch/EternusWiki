###### Noble House
House Atalanta are the wardens of the West in the [[Locations/Sara Dominion]]. They are descended from [[Saint Atalanta]].

Their family artefact is the Rose of Skull and Stone, which was gifted to them by [[Non-Player Characters/Britta of Shrike]]. The current head of house is [[Non-Player Characters/Favon Atalanta]].

House Atalanta is based in [[Locations/Oldegate]].

Their symbol is a bear, and their words are "Tameless, swift, and proud as Wind."
