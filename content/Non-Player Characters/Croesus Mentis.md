###### (he/him) - NPC
Croesus Mentis was an elf from the [[Locations/Sara Dominion]]. Croesus’s time as the oracle had been long awaited, as the previous oracle, his father, had had no oracular powers of his own. Croesus was considered to be one of the most powerful oracles in quite some time, and was most famous for his visions of the Battle of [[Oldegate]]. He married Lady [[Pythia Delphi]], and together, they had one daughter, [[Player Characters/Xenevieve Sara\|Xenavieve Mentis]]. Pythia died when Xenevieve was a young girl, and soon after, Croesus was diagnosed with [[Magic/Spellrot]]. His condition continued to worsen at an alarming rate in the following years, until his eventual death. It is thought that his later visions may have depicted parts of [[Events/Agon#The Rending\|The Rending]], though most of his writings of the time were unintelligible due to the madness caused by his illness. 

Croesus saw his visions in the form of dreams. It is important to note that Pythia also foretold the future, though used painting to do so. Whether she had a prior connection to [[Groups/House Mentis]] is unknown.

He was oracle from 182 CA - 212 CA (30 years).
