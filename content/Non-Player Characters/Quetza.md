###### (he/it) - NPC
Quetza Khan the protector of the Western Khanate in the [[Locations/Daymora Khaganate]], and one of the [[Creatures/Sun Drakes]] created by [[Non-Player Characters/Lathander]]. His serpentine body was covered in large feathers, and compared to the other sun drakes he could fly incredibly quickly.

He was slain by [[Ubtao]] Rex, a [[Creatures/Chimaera (Creatures)\|Chimaera]] general mixed with an [[Creatures/Elemental Titans\|Elemental Titan]]. Witnessing the strength of Ubtao, many people living in the Western Khanate and the [[Hesperides]] shifted to worshipping it instead, leaving the khanate divided and without a protector.
