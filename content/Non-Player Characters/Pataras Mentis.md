Pataras Mentis was a [[Fey]] from the [[Locations/Sara Dominion]]. He was the eldest son of [[Non-Player Characters/Riparia Mentis]], and was brother to [[Non-Player Characters/Rei Mentis]]. The two of them both received visions, though it was Pataras, the eldest, who was set to inherit the role of the oracle. Rei’s visions became more erratic later in life, and caused her much confusion. She is considered by many to have had the first recorded case of [[Spellrot]] in the Sara Dominion, though this is unconfirmed. Before Pataras had a chance to take the position of oracle, he was found dead outside the Mentis estate, having been pushed from the roof by his sister during one of her episodes. 

Pataras saw his visions by watching the clouds. 

He left his magical sword [[Sunray]] to his son, [[Non-Player Characters/Ulysses Mentis]]. It now sits in storage within the [[Shrike's Tine]].
