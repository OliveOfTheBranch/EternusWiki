###### (he/him) - NPC
King Orion Sara was the head of [[Groups/House Sara]] and the father of King [[Non-Player Characters/Theodore Sara]], ruling before him until his death in 196 BA during the [[Events/Battle of Oldegate]]. He was married to Queen [[Minerva Sara]], who died shortly after him during childbirth. While alive, Orion was known for his boyish charm and charisma, and was well liked by most. He had a close friendship with [[Non-Player Characters/Croesus Mentis]], and after taking the throne, he was the one to order that [[Non-Player Characters/Tessera Mentis]] step down as Oracle of Sara, so that Croesus might take the position. When Croesus foretold Orion's death in the Battle of Oldegate, he chose to fight regardless, accepting his fate and dying alongside his countrymen, an act that earnt him great respect.
