###### (she/her) - NPC
Sköll is an [[Ossindra]] werewolf originally from the [[Locations/Daymora Khaganate]], now living in the [[Lupercal]]. She was hand-picked by [[Non-Player Characters/Týr Sara]] to be the deputy of [[Groups/The Pack]]. While Týr was imprisoned by [[Non-Player Characters/Yuphra]], Sköll became the de facto leader alongside [[Hati]].

Old magic taught to her by Týr allows her to transform into a wolf with patterns similar to her magical tattoos at will. To this day, she is the only mortal known to have defeated Týr in melee combat.
