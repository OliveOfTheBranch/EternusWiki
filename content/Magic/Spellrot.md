Spellrot is a non-communicable animatic disease usually affecting sorcerers. Usually, sorcerers have their souls interlinked with the [[Magic/Veil]] such that they can draw on arcane magic at will. However, spellrot causes their soul to be wrapped so tightly within the Veil that using magic draws on their life force rather than the source of the Veil itself. If the affected then continues to use magic, they can experience loss of identity, sanity, and understanding, eventually culminating in death. Attempting to limit the use of magic is the only known way of treating spellrot, as it is incurable.

Many sorcerers alive during the Time of Strife spontaneously contracted spellrot due to the disruption of the Veil and [[Magic/Primordeus]]. However, it is also thought that it could be genetic, due to how common it is within [[Groups/House Mentis]].
