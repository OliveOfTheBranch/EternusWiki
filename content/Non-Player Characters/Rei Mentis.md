###### (she/her) - NPC
Rei Mentis was a [[Creatures/Fey]] from the [[Locations/Sara Dominion]]. She was the youngest daughter of [[Non-Player Characters/Riparia Mentis]], and was sister to [[Non-Player Characters/Pataras Mentis]]. The two of them both received visions, though it was her older brother Pataras who was set to inherit the role of the oracle. Rei’s visions became more erratic later in life, and caused her much confusion. She is considered by many to have had the first recorded case of [[Magic/Spellrot]] in the Sara Dominion, though this is unconfirmed. She claimed to have seen many visions of birds without wings, which seem to have predicted the fate of her brother. Before Pataras had a chance to take the position of oracle, he was found dead outside the Mentis estate, having been pushed from the roof by his sister during one of her episodes. Rei was the Oracle of Sara for only three years, before she was replaced by her nephew, [[Non-Player Characters/Ulysses Mentis]]. 

Rei’s visions came to her as vivid hallucinations. 

She was oracle from 145 GA - 148 GA (3 years).
