###### (he/him) - NPC
King Theodore Sara is the current ruling monarch of the [[Sara Dominion]]. He is a human, and is also known as the "Cursèd King".

Theo has short brown hair and is fairly lithe, being a hunter.

Theo once had possession of the [[Ladon]], which was given to him by [[Dalys Meleager]]. On her eighteenth birthday, Theo bequeathed the artefact onto his daughter, [[Victoria Sara]].

As a Sara, he is descended from the goddess [[Clio Sara]], who began the royal line of [[House Sara]].
