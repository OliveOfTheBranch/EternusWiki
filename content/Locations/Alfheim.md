###### Continent
Alfheim is the eastern continent of Eternus. Western Alfheim is home to the Free Cities of the [[Impious Alliance]], such as [[Locations/Waterdeep]] and [[Neverwinter]]. Meanwhile, eastern Alfheim is considered the east edge of the world, as the materiality of the continent begins to blend into the [[Locations/Feywild]].
