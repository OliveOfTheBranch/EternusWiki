###### (he/it) - NPC
The primordial god of the hunt, Typhon was an apex predator and creator of the first monsters, known as chimaera. He roamed the Hunting Grounds of East Ethrys, wreaking carnage and destruction in his wake, until he himself was hunted and killed by the mortal [[Non-Player Characters/Clio Sara]] and her [[Groups/Hunters of Sara\|hunters]].
