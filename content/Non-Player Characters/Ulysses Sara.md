###### (he/him) - NPC
Ulysses Sara was the husband of [[Non-Player Characters/Clio Sara]], and the first king of the [[Locations/Sara Dominion]]. 
He was born in the northern [[Hunting Grounds]], shortly before [[Events/Agon#The First Agon\|The First Agon]], and around a time when the north was already beginning to unite under the rule of his father, the [[King of the North]]. He lived in a town that is now the city of [[Locations/Chimaera]], and was there when the [[Groups/Hunters of Sara]] slayed [[Non-Player Characters/Typhon]]. 

Ulysses married Clio Sara, giving her a legitimate claim to the north, and stood by her side as she united her empire, forming the Sara Dominion as we know it today. Together, they had one son, [[Aegon Sara]], who ruled after them. 

Ulysses helped to write the [[Items/Predator's Bible]], alongside [[Saint Orion]] and [[Non-Player Characters/Aureon Mentis\|Saint Mentis]]. He is also considered by many to be the inventor of modern magic, as he is credited with the discovery of [[Magic/Veil]] magic. After his retirement in 50 GA, he became known as the Arcana, creating a title that would be used there on for retired consorts.
