###### (she/her) - NPC
Lorelei Mentis was an elf from the [[Locations/Sara Dominion]]. After taking her position by force, [[Non-Player Characters/Desdemona Mentis]] had three children: Loralei, [[Non-Player Characters/Helena Mentis]], and [[Non-Player Characters/Adonis Mentis]]. Lorelei heard whispers of the future whenever the world was silent, Helena could tell the future using a set of carved bones, and Adonis saw visions flickering in fire. Lorelei was the first to take the role of oracle, though was diagnosed with [[Magic/Spellrot]] only five years into her role. She left [[Locations/Chimaera]] soon after, and was not heard from again. 

Lorelei heard whispers of the future whenever she was alone in her house. 

She was oracle from 666 GA - 671 GA (5 years).
