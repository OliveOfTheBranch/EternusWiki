###### Sovereign Pantheon
The Court of Pain is unlike any other god. Instead of an individual taking the [[Magic/Animus]] of a god as their own soul, the Court of Pain used magic to trap the animus [[Non-Player Characters/The Wanderer]] within an the [[Locations/Godflow Engine]]. This allowed them to share and experiment with divine power without having to bind the animus to themselves. The court was founded by the witch Tasha, and is based in the flying city of [[Locations/Sigil]]. Though the Court of Pain has many members, its primary members and keepers of godly power are known as sovereigns: 
- [[Non-Player Characters/Tasha]], the Grand Sovereign
- [[Non-Player Characters/Aureon Mentis]], Sovereign of Knowledge
- [[Non-Player Characters/Xaeres]], Sovereign of War
- [[Non-Player Characters/Aryas]], Uncanny Sovereign
- [[Non-Player Characters/Carter of Marwin]], Grazed Sovereign
