###### Fey Domain
Archheart is the capital city of the [[Locations/Feywild]] and home of the [[Seelie Fey\|Seelie]] Court (now the [[United Court]]). Its [[Archfey]] is [[Non-Player Characters/Titania]], the Fey Queen. The city sits in amongst a sprawling forest, with houses in every tree and beds in every hollow. In the centre stands a huge marble palace that looks out over the domain.
