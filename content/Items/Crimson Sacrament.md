###### Divine Focus
A Crimson Sacrament is a sort of bowl used in [[Locations/Dreygaard\|Dregaardian]] churches for ritualistic reasons. It is used to collect blood for [[Non-Player Characters/The Sanguine Lord]]. Many depictions of the Sanguine Lord include a Crimson Sacrament, usually held in two of his four arms.
