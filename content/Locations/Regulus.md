###### City
Regulus is a city located in the southern savanna of the [[Locations/Sara Dominion]]. It was the home of [[Saint Nemea]], and is still to this day inhabited by [[Groups/House Nemea]]. It is also the home of [[Groups/House Taurus]], and part of the Bas'Taurus region.
