###### Island Group
Vëlheim, or the Vëlheim Isles, is a group of islands northeast of [[Locations/Dreygaard]]. 

It is inhabited by the [[Creatures/Vëllan]], a population that is indigenous to northern Dreygaard and the Vëlheim Isles. The Vëllan were created by [[Non-Player Characters/The Wanderer]], and rarely stray from their ancestral home, despite the harsh conditions. The islands are also home to a variety of dangerous creatures, including [[Hags]], [[Vampires]], and [[Creatures/Ols]]. Dreygaard have led many expeditions here to hunt the Vëllan, as they condemn them for witchcraft, an act of heresy against their god, [[Non-Player Characters/The Sanguine Lord]].
