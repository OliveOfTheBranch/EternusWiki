###### (he/it) - NPC
Aryas is a changeling [[Ol]] originally from [[Locations/Chimaera]], but has since moved to [[Sigil]] to become a member of [[Groups/The Court of Pain]]. [[Aureon]]’s protégé, Aryas keeps the court's enemies in line. When a warning doesn’t suffice, he has free reign to use his horrid powers to ensure that they never speak ill of the court again.

His sister is [[Player Characters/Silva]], a member of [[Eternus Victoria]]. He grew up with her in [[Saint Meleager's Home for Children]].
