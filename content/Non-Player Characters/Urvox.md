###### (he/they) - NPC
The primordial god of speech, Urvox the Convoker was one of the [[Groups/Cloaked Gods]] who spoke the first humans and dragons into existence. As a paragon of balance and equilibrium, he created two equally powerful armies—each led by a dragon god—to represent good and evil, and fight to the death in an attempt to see who would win, known as the [[Convoker’s War]].
