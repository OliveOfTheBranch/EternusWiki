###### Plant
Blood Lilies are a type of lily, endemic to the Gaard region of [[Locations/Dreygaard]]. They are the national flower, appearing on the Dreygaardian flag, and were rumoured to have been created by [[Non-Player Characters/The Sanguine Lord]] himself. The flowers are a deep red colour, and produce a nectar with anticoagulant properties. The flower itself is mildly toxic, but is consumed ritualistically in Dreygaard during certain times of the year.
