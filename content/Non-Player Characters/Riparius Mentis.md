Riparius Mentis was an elf from the [[Locations/Sara Dominion]]. As [[Non-Player Characters/Pontus Mentis]]'s eldest son, Riparius was due to become oracle once his father gave up the position. He and his younger sister, [[Non-Player Characters/Eldath Mentis]], gained experience by travelling the country in their youth, and learned what the people needed to escape the Time of Strife. Unfortunately, Riparius spontaneously contracted [[Spellrot]] and died young, leaving Eldath to serve as oracle in his place. 

Riparius saw the future in the glimmering reflections of people’s eyes.
