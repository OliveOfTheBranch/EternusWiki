###### (she/her) - NPC
Alexis Mentis was an elf from the [[Locations/Sara Dominion]]. She became the Oracle of Sara at 18, as soon as she was old enough to take on the role. She read the future by spilling blood, and was rumoured to worship [[Non-Player Characters/The Sanguine Lord]], though this remains unproven. Eventually she was replaced by her younger sister, [[Non-Player Characters/Irene Mentis]] who was thought to be more suitable for the job, and she moved up north to a border town. She married a [[Dreygaard\|Dreygaardian]] human, and had a daughter, [[Non-Player Characters/Belladonna Mentis]], as well as a son, [[Non-Player Characters/Dreyr Mentis-Strzyga]], who was born from an affair with a Dreygaardian prince, and kept secret.

Alexis read the future in the spattering of spilled blood. 

She was oracle from 698 GA - 720 GA (22 years).
