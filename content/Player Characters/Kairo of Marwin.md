###### (she/her) - Played by Livinya
Kairo is a [[Satyr]] from [[Marwin]] in the [[Locations/Feywild]]. She was a part of the adventuring party [[Groups/Eternus Victoria]], and is the sister of [[Non-Player Characters/Carter of Marwin]], the Grazed Sovereign. 

After coming back from the dead, she briefly joined a monastery, before ending up in [[Locations/Chimaera]] at the [[Witchlight Carnival]], where Eternus Victoria was formed.
